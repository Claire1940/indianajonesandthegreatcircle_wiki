To begin this intriguing adventure, you'll first need to progress far enough into the game's main story to unlock the Sukhothai Village area. Once there, your primary objective is to locate and infiltrate the Fascist Armory.

<Section icon={Target} title="Infiltrating the Fascist Armory and Gathering Clues">
Gaining access to the Fascist Armory requires a bit of cunning. The main entrance is typically blocked by a barbed-wire fence, but a convenient hole will allow Indiana Jones to slide through. After entering, navigate through the ruins to reach the small camp where the Armory is situated. Your destination within the camp is the tallest building.

<Callout type="warning" title="Security Alert">
The Fascist Armory is teeming with enemies. While combat is an option, a stealthy approach is highly recommended to avoid unnecessary confrontations and conserve resources. Consider using the Royal Army disguise if available.
</Callout>

Once inside the main building, head to the second floor. Here, you'll discover the initial pieces of the "Monkey Business" puzzle. You'll find a bulletin board with a "Monkey Warning" and a "Keyring Note" by the door, indicating that the Equipment Room Keys are missing. Additionally, pick up "Francesco's Note." These items collectively trigger the start of the mystery and provide crucial context.

### Key Clues to Collect

| Item             | Location                           | Significance                                      | Adventure Points |
| :--------------- | :--------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------ | :--------------- |
| **Keyring Note** | By the Equipment Room door         | Confirms missing keys, points to monkey involvement | 50               |
| **Monkey Warning** | Bulletin board, second floor       | Alert about local monkeys stealing shiny objects  | -                |
| **Francesco's Note** | Near the Monkey Warning            | Additional context about the situation            | -                |

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Tracking Down the Mischievous Monkeys">
With the notes in hand, Indiana deduces that the local monkeys are likely responsible for the missing Equipment Room Key. These primates are known for their penchant for shiny objects, making them prime suspects. Your next task is to track these furry culprits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Exit the Armory">
    Leave the Fascist Armory compound through the same hole in the barbed-wire fence you used to enter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Ruins">
    Once outside the fence, turn left and proceed over a wall of collapsed bricks that are part of the surrounding ruins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cross the Swamp">
    Continue forward from the ruined wall and wade through the swampy waters to your right. This path will lead you towards a small clearing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate the Monkeys">
    In the clearing, you'll find a group of monkeys playing with a collection of stolen items from the Fascist Armory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success">
The monkeys' hideout is typically not far from the Armory. Keep an eye out for a small rock within their clearing; the Equipment Room Key will be prominently displayed on top of it, amidst other pilfered goods like a pistol and books.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Unlocking the Equipment Room and Claiming Rewards">
Once you've retrieved the Equipment Room Key from the monkeys, it's time to return to the Fascist Armory to unlock your well-earned prize. Head back to the main compound, specifically to the equipment room located near where you first started the mystery.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reward Highlights">
    - **250 Adventure Points**: Contributes to your overall progression.
    - **Slugger II Adventure Book**: Provides a significant gameplay advantage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Slugger II Benefits">
    - **Increased damage** from charge-up attacks.
    - Costs 900 Adventure Points to read.
    - Enhance melee combat effectiveness.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Use the newly acquired key to open the equipment room door. Inside, you'll find not only the satisfaction of completing the "Monkey Business" Mystery but also valuable resources. The primary reward is the **Slugger II Adventure Book**, which, once read, grants you increased damage from charge-up attacks. This can be a significant boost for melee-focused players.

<Callout type="info">
The "Monkey Business" Mystery is considered complete once you open the equipment room door. Remember to collect all items inside, especially the Slugger II book, before continuing your adventures.
</Callout>
