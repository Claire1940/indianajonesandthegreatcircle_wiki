<Section icon={Target} title="Fortune's Reach: Step-by-Step Puzzle Solution">
Solving the Fortune's Reach Mystery involves a clever little puzzle that requires attention to detail. The core of the puzzle revolves around matching dates and cloud types to derive a numerical code. Follow these steps to successfully crack the mystery:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Meteorological Station">
    Head to the Meteorological Station, situated northwest of the Gizeh Workers' Area. This is where you'll find the primary clues for the puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find the Cloud Status Logbook">
    Inside the station, search for the Cloud Status Logbook. This logbook contains a series of dates and corresponding cloud types.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify Cloud Atlas Page Numbers">
    You will need to cross-reference the cloud types from the logbook with the Cloud Atlas Page numbers. The key is to convert the identified cloud types into their numerical equivalents. The relevant dates are 13.10, 09.10, 16.10, and 14.10.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Match Cloud Types to Numbers">
    Match the cloud types found in the logbook (Cirrus, Stratus, Cirrus, and Cumulonimbus) to their numerical values. This conversion will yield the four-digit code.
    <Callout type="success" title="The Code">
    The correct numerical conversion for the cloud types will lead to the code: **0609**.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Open the Chest">
    Input the code **0609** into the locked chest associated with the Fortune's Reach Mystery. The chest is typically found within the Meteorological Station area.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
