  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Exploration Books">

  <Step num="3" title="Clear Pending Mysteries">

  <Step num="4" title="The Secret Vault">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Map} title="The Order of Giants DLC Integration">

If you own the Premium Edition or the Story DLC, "The Order of Giants" seamlessly integrates into your post-game world. This expansion sends Indy back to Rome and the Vatican to uncover the secrets of the Nephilim.

**DLC Completion Checklist:**
- **118 New Journal Notes**: Specifically located within the Rome and Villa Pia districts.
- **7 Mithraic Artifacts**: These function similarly to Lost Artifacts and are required for the "The Seven Grades" achievement.
- **New Mysteries**: Solve the "A Mystery Begins" fieldwork by visiting Father Ricci near the Fountain of Confession.
- **Pet the Cat**: A unique interaction at Via Giulia that is required for 100% DLC completion.
