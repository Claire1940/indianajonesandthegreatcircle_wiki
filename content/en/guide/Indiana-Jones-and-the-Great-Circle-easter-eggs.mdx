### Giza: Echoes of the Ark

The developers have gone to great lengths to recreate the atmosphere and specific moments from *Raiders of the Lost Ark* in the Egyptian setting.

| Location Detail | Movie Reference | Game Interaction |
|:----------------|:----------------|:-----------------|
| **BV38 Plane**  | The plane designed to carry the Ark of the Covenant in *Raiders of the Lost Ark*. | Visible on the ground just before landing in Giza. |
| **Workers' Chanting** | The distinct chanting heard before Indy enters the map room in *Raiders*. | Head northwest of Giza, position yourself correctly, and you'll hear the exact same chants. |
| **Fine Leather Jackets** | A running joke from *Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade* video game and other LucasArts titles. | A trader in the town's market sells "fine leather jackets," allowing you to engage in the classic dialogue. |

Shanghai brings thrilling airplane sequences with some fantastic hidden details.

During the "Lost in the Past" quest in Sukhotai, Indy experiences a bizarre dream sequence that pays homage to a cult classic horror game.

<CardGrid cols={1}>
