<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Cogwheel Placement">

To successfully complete the **indiana jones and the great circle blessed pearl** quest, you must follow a specific order of operations. The gears are interchangeable, but their placement in the slots is rigid.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize the Left Mechanism">
    Pick up the large gear from the floor and place it in the central hub. Locate two small gears and place them in the upper circles. This will complete the connection to the first stone head.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Lower the First Head">
    Pull the manual lever located to the left of the gear train. You will see the stone head descend. Once it reaches the bottom, the first phase is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Retrieve the Hidden Cog">
    Go underneath the wooden scaffolding and climb the wall to reach the upper ledge. Grab the final small gear part from the ancient pedestal. This is required for the second half of the puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transfer to the Right Side">
    Remove all gears from the left mechanism. Move them to the right-hand side. Place two large gears in the main hubs and three small gears in the connecting circles (two on top, one on bottom).
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Activate the Water Flow">
    Before the right lever will work, you must power it. Climb the ledge above the right mechanism and look for a hanging rope or lever. Use Indy's strength to yank it, starting the water flow over the wheel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved">
Once the water is flowing and the gears are in place, pull the final lever. The second head will descend, opening the path to the Blessed Pearl.
</Callout>

| Problem | Likely Cause | Solution |
