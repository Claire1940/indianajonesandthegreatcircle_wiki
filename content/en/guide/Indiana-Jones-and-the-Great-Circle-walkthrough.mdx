<Section icon={BookOpen} title="Marshall College: The Museum Mystery">

After the jungle prologue, the scene shifts to Marshall College. A late-night break-in at the museum by a mysterious giant sets the main plot in motion. You must assist Marcus Brody in identifying what was stolen and finding clues about the thief's identity.

The primary objective here is to reorganize a ransacked display case. You must match artifacts to their correct cultural origins based on their visual style and Indy's descriptions.

| Artifact Item | Correct Origin | Placement Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bastet Statue** | Ancient Egyptian | Left side of the display |
| **Terra Cotta Relief** | Babylonian (Iraq) | Center-back section |
| **Ugaritic Letters** | Syrian (Ras Shamra) | Right side of the display |
| **Funerary Mask** | Egyptian (Gold) | Front-center section |

Once the puzzle is complete, you discover that a specific cat mummy is missing. Investigation of the crime scene reveals a **Vatican Pendant** dropped by the giant. This leads Indy to pack his essentials—including his journal and whip—and head to Rome.

<Callout type="warning" title="Missing Items">
Don't rush out of the office! Ensure you grab the **Adventure Journal** from the suitcase and the **Bullwhip** from the desk. These are required to progress through the next missions.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Infiltrating Vatican City">

The **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle walkthrough** continues in Rome, where Indy meets his old friend Antonio. To move freely within the Vatican, you must adopt a disguise. Antonio provides a clerical suit that allows you to walk past most guards in non-restricted areas.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Disguise">
    Put on the priest's outfit in Antonio's office. This allows you to explore public areas of the Vatican without raising immediate suspicion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Library Secret">
    Head to the library and look for a hidden switch behind a bookshelf. This leads to an underground laboratory used by Father Kenzo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Underwater Navigation">
    Dive into the flooded sections of the laboratory to find a lever that opens the gate for Antonio. Watch your oxygen levels!
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Post Office Quest">
    Visit Ernesto at the Vatican Post Office. You will need to "earn" enough currency to purchase a camera, which is essential for documenting clues.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Stealth Strategy">
While in the clerical suit, you are safe in public zones. However, if you enter a "Restricted Area" (indicated on screen), guards will attack on sight regardless of your clothes. Use bottles to lure guards away from doorways.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Inscriptions and the Tower of Nicholas I">

With the camera in hand, your task is to find and photograph four specific inscriptions hidden around the Vatican. These inscriptions provide the coordinates for the "Underworld" mentioned in Father Kenzo's notes.

**Required Inscription Locations:**
- **The Courtyard Wall**: Located near the secondary guard post.
- **The Library Archway**: High above the main entrance to the restricted archives.
- **The Garden Statue Base**: Found behind the hedge maze in the papal gardens.
- **The Tower Base**: Located on the exterior of the Tower of Nicholas I.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat and Skill Progression">

Combat in *The Great Circle* is a mix of visceral hand-to-hand brawling and tactical tool usage. While Indy can use firearms, ammo is scarce, and loud noises attract reinforcements. Mastering the melee system is vital for survival.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Brawler Skill">
    - **Increased melee damage**
    - Faster punch combinations
    - Essential for guard takedowns
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lucky Hat">
    - **Second chance mechanic**
    - Allows Indy to recover after a knockout
    - Requires picking up the dropped Fedora
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stamina Boost">
    - **Longer sprint duration**
    - More effective dodges
    - Vital for escaping large groups
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

When engaged in a fight, use the **Charge Attack** (hold the attack button) to break a guard's block. If you are outnumbered, use the whip to disarm enemies or trip them, giving you a window to escape or perform a finisher.

| Enemy Type | Weakness | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Guard** | Stealth Takedown | Sneak behind or use bottle distraction |
| **Blackshirt Officer** | Whip Disarm | Disarm their pistol before engaging in melee |
| **Guard Dog** | Meat / Distraction | Throw food or a bottle to divert their path |

<Callout type="warning" title="Health Management">
Health does not fully regenerate automatically. You must use bandages or consume food items like apples and donuts found in the environment to restore your HP bars.
</Callout>
