<Section icon={Shield} title="First Encounter: The Marshall College Museum">

The first time you meet Locus is in the familiar halls of Marshall College. This encounter serves as a narrative introduction rather than a traditional boss fight. You will find yourself in the museum room, attempting to protect the cat statue Indy intends to return to the school.

In this specific instance, you actually **cannot beat Locus**. The game uses this fight to establish Locus as a superior physical force. You may land a few punches, but the sequence is designed to lead into a cutscene where Locus overpowers Indy. 

| Action | Result | Narrative Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Punching Locus** | Minimal Damage | Shows his resilience |
| **Getting Grabbed** | Cutscene Trigger | Locus steals the idol |
| **Chase Sequence** | Medallion Retrieval | Sets up the Vatican hunt |

<Callout type="info">

<Section icon={Target} title="Final Encounter: The Sukhothai Arena">

The final showdown in Sukhothai is the most difficult. The arena is small, and Locus is significantly more aggressive. He will lunge from across the stage, and his hit detection is wider than it appears visually. To **defeat Locus** here, you must master the "Circle and Backpedal" method.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Maintain the Perimeter">
    Constantly move in a large circle around the arena. Do not get backed into a corner, as Locus's grab range is deceptive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bait the Lunge">
    Wait for Locus to lunge forward or attempt a grab. Dodge backward twice to ensure you are outside his reach.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Counter-Punch">
    Once his animation ends, move in for exactly **two punches**. If you attempt a third, he will counter-punch you instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset and Repeat">
    Immediately dodge away after your second hit to reset your stamina and prepare for his next move.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Locus Move | Visual Tell | Recommended Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Overhead Slam** | Raises both arms | Side-dodge and jab |
| **Bear Hug Grab** | Lowers shoulders/lunges | Double back-dodge |
| **Standard Hook** | Pulls one arm back | Parry or block |

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Warning">
Do not attempt to use your revolver. In these boss encounters, firearms are largely ineffective or restricted. Relying on your gun will leave you vulnerable to a quick knockdown.
</Callout>
