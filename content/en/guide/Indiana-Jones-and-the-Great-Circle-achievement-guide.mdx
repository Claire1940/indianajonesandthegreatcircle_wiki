<Section icon={Target} title="The Ultimate Collectible Hunt">

The bulk of your time in the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle achievement guide** will be spent hunting for collectibles. The game features an excellent tracking system, but you must be proactive.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Purchase Mystery Books">
    Buy books from vendors using money or medicine bottles. These books mark the locations of Mysteries, Journal Notes, and Photographs on your map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Shutterbug Grind">
    Whenever the camera icon appears on your UI, stop and take a photo. You need 50 unique photographs for the "Shutterbug" achievement and to earn Adventure Points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Medicine Bottle Economy">
    Collect medicine bottles and take them to local pharmacies. You can trade 10 bottles for rare Adventure Books that unlock essential player abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ancient Relics and Endings">
    Collect all Ancient Relics (found in the three main hubs). Do NOT complete the final relic puzzle until you have finished all other collectible achievements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Ability and Adventure Points">

To unlock all abilities, you need a massive amount of Adventure Points. These are earned by completing fieldwork, taking photos, and finding lost artifacts.

- **Learn 1 Ability**: 20G
- **Learn 10 Abilities**: 40G
- **Learn All Abilities**: 70G

You must buy every Adventure Book from vendors to even have the option to learn these skills. If you are short on money, prioritize the boxing rings, as they offer significant rewards and unique books.
