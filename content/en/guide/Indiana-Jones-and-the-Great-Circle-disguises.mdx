### How Disguises Work

Successfully using a disguise involves more than just putting it on. Here's a quick breakdown:

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Infiltration">
    - **Access Restricted Areas**: Walk past guards and enter zones that would otherwise trigger immediate combat.
    - **Blend In**: Stay out of direct line of sight and avoid suspicious actions to maintain cover.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Detection">
    - **Close Proximity**: Enemies might recognize Indy if he gets too close or stays in their direct gaze for too long.
    - **Hostile Actions**: Attacking, using the whip aggressively, or being spotted with a weapon will instantly break cover.
  </Card>

### General Tips for Using Disguises

- **Observe Patrols**: Before entering a disguised area, watch enemy patrol routes to plan your movements.
- **Avoid Running**: Walking or moving at a normal pace is less suspicious than sprinting.
- **Use Cover**: Even when disguised, utilize environmental cover to avoid prolonged exposure to guards.
- **Silent Takedowns**: If you must neutralize an enemy, ensure it's a silent takedown from behind, away from other patrols. The "Throttle" Adventure Book upgrade is excellent for this, allowing Indy to whip-pull unaware lightweight and medium-weight enemies.

<Section icon={Target} title="Vatican Disguises: Locations and Uses">

The Vatican is one of the initial major hubs where disguises become crucial for navigating complex and heavily guarded areas. Here, you'll encounter various personnel, from museum staff to military guards, each requiring a specific approach.

### Vatican Uniforms Overview

| Disguise Type         | Primary Location         | Use Case                                  | Effectiveness | Key Areas                                        |
|-----------------------|--------------------------|-------------------------------------------|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Museum Staff Uniform** | Apostolic Palace, Museum Wing | Blending with museum personnel, accessing staff-only areas | Medium        | Art galleries, archives, back corridors          |
| **Military Guard Uniform** | Belvedere Courtyard, Military Zone | Infiltrating heavily guarded military checkpoints | High          | Courtyard, secure research facilities, underground paths |
| **Janitor's Overalls** | Vatican Cellars, Maintenance Areas | Moving through service tunnels and lower levels | Low-Medium    | Basements, ventilation shafts, hidden passages   |

<Callout type="warning" title="Vatican Guard Behavior">
Vatican guards are particularly alert to anyone lingering too long or acting out of place. Be quick and purposeful in your movements, even when disguised.
</Callout>

### Obtaining Vatican Disguises

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Museum Staff Uniform">
    This uniform can often be found in staff locker rooms or storage areas within the **Apostolic Palace** or the **Museum Wing**. Look for less-trafficked offices or utility closets. Sometimes, a specific mission objective will lead you directly to one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Military Guard Uniform">
    The military uniform is typically located in the **Belvedere Courtyard's Military Zone** or within secure barracks. You might need to stealthily knock out a guard and take their uniform, or locate a supply crate containing one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Janitor's Overalls">
    These are usually found in the **Vatican Cellars** or other maintenance areas. During the "A Savage Discovery" Mystery, you might find an opportunity to acquire these to access hidden passages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Gizeh Disguises: Desert Infiltration">

In the arid landscapes of Gizeh, Indy will face different factions, primarily Nazi forces and local workers. Disguises here are critical for navigating excavation sites, military camps, and ancient tombs without raising alarms.

### Gizeh Uniforms Overview

| Disguise Type         | Primary Location         | Use Case                                  | Effectiveness | Key Areas                                        |
|-----------------------|--------------------------|-------------------------------------------|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Nazi Uniform**      | Nazi Compound, Dig Sites | Infiltrating Nazi-controlled excavation sites and bases | High          | Voss' office, Great Pyramid Excavation Site, Sphinx area |
| **Worker's Attire**   | Gizeh Workers' Area, Camps | Blending with local laborers, accessing non-military zones | Medium        | Supply depots, local villages, outer dig perimeters |
| **Archaeologist's Outfit** | Research Tents, Small Digs | Gaining access to specific research tents or less secure dig areas | Low-Medium    | Smaller archaeological finds, temporary camps    |

<Callout type="success" title="Strategic Advantage">
The Nazi Uniform is arguably the most impactful disguise in Gizeh, opening up many critical paths through heavily fortified enemy positions.
</Callout>

### Acquiring Gizeh Disguises

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Nazi Uniform">
    This uniform is a high-priority acquisition. It can be found in the **Nazi Compound**, particularly in barracks or the building housing Voss' office. Sometimes, you can find it after a stealth takedown of an isolated guard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Worker's Attire">
    These are commonly found in the **Gizeh Workers' Area** or scattered around various camps. Look for laundry lines, storage sheds, or tents where laborers might change.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Archaeologist's Outfit">
    Often located in smaller research tents or temporary excavation sites outside the main Nazi presence. These are less effective against Nazi patrols but can help with local authorities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>

The lush, ancient ruins of Sukhothai present a unique set of challenges, often involving hidden camps and patrols amidst dense jungle. Disguises here are vital for navigating treacherous environments and avoiding detection by various factions.

### Sukhothai Uniforms Overview

| Disguise Type         | Primary Location         | Use Case                                  | Effectiveness | Key Areas                                        |
|-----------------------|--------------------------|-------------------------------------------|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Local Fisherman's Clothes** | Flooded Huts, Riverside Villages | Blending with local populace, traversing riverine areas | Medium        | Water routes, small settlements, market areas    |
| **Voss's Camp Guard Uniform** | Voss's Camp Barracks, Outposts | Infiltrating Voss's highly secure jungle camp | High          | Barracks, armories, command centers within the camp |
| **Monk's Robes**      | Wat Mahathat, Temples    | Accessing sacred temple grounds or religious ceremonies | Low-Medium    | Wat Mahathat, hidden shrines, peaceful temple areas |

<Callout type="info" title="Environmental Blending">
The dense foliage and intricate waterways of Sukhothai make it an ideal region for stealth. Disguises, combined with environmental cover, can make Indy almost invisible.
</Callout>

### How to Find Sukhothai Disguises

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Local Fisherman's Clothes">
    These can be found in the numerous **flooded huts** or small **riverside villages** scattered throughout Sukhothai. Check clotheslines or storage chests within these areas.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Voss's Camp Guard Uniform">
    To obtain this crucial uniform, you'll need to penetrate **Voss's Camp**. Look in the barracks or guard posts for opportunities to acquire it, often after a stealthy takedown.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monk's Robes">
    These are typically located within or near **temple grounds**, such as **Wat Mahathat**. They are less for direct combat infiltration and more for cultural immersion or accessing specific religious sites.
  </Step>
</Steps>
