<Section icon={Target} title="Main Story + Side Content: Extending Your Expedition">
For adventurers who enjoy a more comprehensive journey, *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle* offers a wealth of side content that significantly extends playtime. Engaging with "fieldwork" missions, hunting down collectibles, and solving optional puzzles can easily add another 10 to 15 hours to your total. This brings the "Main + Extras" category to an average of **25 to 26 hours**.

Side quests, often referred to as fieldwork, are seamlessly integrated into the game world. They don't always appear as obvious markers on your map, encouraging organic exploration and discovery. For instance, an early main quest might involve taking pictures of inscriptions, and an NPC could then suggest finding more, turning it into a fieldwork task. Similarly, discovering a random note could trigger a new investigation.

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Miss Out on Fieldwork">
Fieldwork missions and collectibles are crucial for character progression. They often reward Adventure Points, which are essential for unlocking powerful skills via Adventure Books, and can also provide medicine bottles to increase health and stamina.
</Callout>

For the dedicated completionist, *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle* offers a substantial challenge. Fully completing the game, which involves tracking down every collectible, solving every optional puzzle, and finishing all fieldwork, can take an estimated **39 to 40 hours or more**. The game's leveling system is tied to discovery and looting. This design encourages thorough exploration if you aim to max out Indy's capabilities.
