<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Navigating Marshall College">

To efficiently clear the first part of the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle mission list**, follow these specific steps to solve the initial mysteries.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Museum Break-in">
    Follow the sounds of the intruder through the museum. Use your flashlight to navigate the darkened corridors and interact with the disturbed displays to trigger Indy's observations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Circular Desk Puzzle">
    Before entering the historical display, investigate the circular desk. You will find the Faculty Card here, which is required to access the restricted archives in the basement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Custodian's Path">
    After solving the light-reflection puzzle in the main hall, head through the open door to the custodian's room. Check the far shelves for hidden notes regarding the "Giant" who broke in.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Academic Investigation">
    Head to the second floor. Enter Indy's classroom to find three separate notes (Teacher's Notes, Stool Note, and Shirley's Letter). These provide flavor text about Indy's life as a professor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Clues">
    Proceed to the lounge and grab the Comic Book and Baseball Card. Finally, return to Indy's office to collect the Giant's Pendant and the Cat Mummy photograph to conclude the prologue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
