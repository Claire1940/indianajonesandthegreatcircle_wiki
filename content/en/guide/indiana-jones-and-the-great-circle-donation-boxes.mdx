<Section icon={Target} title="Locating Medicine Bottles and Vendors">
Finding medicine bottles requires thorough exploration of each semi-open world map. These aren't always in plain sight and often require a keen eye or a bit of puzzle-solving to reach. Once you've amassed a good collection, the next step is to find the vendors who will accept them.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Explore Thoroughly">
    Scour every corner of the game's maps. Medicine bottles can be hidden in ruins, abandoned structures, secret passages, or even underwater areas. Keep an eye out for any sparkling objects or interactive elements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Pamphlets">
    Visit vendors in each major hub or map. They often sell pamphlets that can reveal the locations of collectibles, including medicine bottles. While not strictly necessary, these can save you significant time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify Vendor Locations">
    Specific vendors in each map are designated to exchange medicine bottles for health and stamina upgrades. These are usually static NPCs found in safe zones or main camps. Their locations are typically marked on your map once discovered, or mentioned in dialogue.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Hand in Medicine Bottles">
    Approach the vendor and interact with them to initiate the exchange. You'll typically need a certain number of medicine bottles for each upgrade. The cost usually increases with each subsequent upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>
