<Section icon={Target} title="Locus's Intervention and the Ark's New Sanctuary">
In the climax of **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle ending**, after Voss is defeated, Locus takes decisive action. Recognizing the immense danger the Ark and its powers pose in the wrong hands, he instructs Indy and Gina to abandon ship. Locus then commandeers the Ark, activating the stones to open a portal and transport the vessel to a location where "no one can find it again." This act ensures the Ark's safety and prevents its misuse by future adversaries.

The game's narrative strongly suggests that Locus, as a member of the ancient Nephilim Order — the protectors of the stones — understands their true nature and how to wield them responsibly. His successful teleportation of the Ark, without the destructive side effects witnessed with Voss, underscores this point.
