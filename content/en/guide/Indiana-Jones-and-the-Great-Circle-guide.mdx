<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Key Puzzle Solutions by Region">

Puzzles are the heart of the Great Circle experience. Many of these require you to use Indy’s camera to identify clues or your journal to cross-reference historical data.

**Vatican: Fountain of Confession**
This is the first major roadblock for many players. After returning the parchment to Antonio, head to the gardens. Turn both dragon statues to face the fountain. Whip the central dragon and rotate the fountain cube. You must solve two sides: the baptism side (align the man and boy with the water ring) and the boat side (rotate rings to clear the path).

**Gizeh: Chamber of Resonance**
Located under the Great Pyramid, this puzzle requires you to photograph Adamic tablets. If tablets are missing, crawl through the low tunnels and use the two-handed mace to break weak walls. Once all photos are taken, select them in this specific order: **Heed > The Lord's > Oath > Protect > The secret > Of the name.**

**Himalayas: Kummetz Cipher**
Inside the KMS Kummetz, you must find four code wheels. Input the Captain's Code (JÖGM) into the machine. This outputs the word "BLUT." Cross-reference this on the room's conversion tables to get the numerical code **3666** for the exit door.

<Callout type="warning" title="Camera Clues">
If you are stuck on a puzzle, pull out your camera. Indy will often provide a verbal hint or highlight a specific interactive object that is necessary for progress.
</Callout>

**Sukhothai: Khmer Cogwheels**
Scattered around the Thai hub are 10 Khmer Cogwheels. You must collect all of them to solve the final gear puzzle west of Voss' Camp. Completing these gear trains rewards you with Ancient Relics and progress toward the "Gear Head" achievement.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Adventure Books and Essential Upgrades">

Adventure Books are the primary progression system. You find the books in the world, then spend Adventure Points (earned through photos and discovery) to unlock permanent buffs.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Lucky Hat I">
    - **Cost**: 125 AP
    - **Effect**: Gives Indy a second chance in combat if he can reach his fallen hat.
    - **Priority**: Essential for beginners.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Moxie I-III">
    - **Cost**: Medicine Bottles
    - **Effect**: Permanently increases Indy's total stamina bar.
    - **Priority**: High. Stamina is used for everything.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Throttle">
    - **Cost**: 200 AP
    - **Effect**: Stealth-takedown medium-weight enemies using the whip pull.
    - **Priority**: Stealth players.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

**Top Progression Tips:**
1.  **Buy Map Books First**: Vendors like Ernesto (Vatican) and Tongdang (Sukhothai) sell books that reveal the locations of other books and relics on your map.
2.  **Collect Medicine Bottles**: Unlike AP, health and stamina upgrades (Shaping Up and Moxie) require regional Medicine Bottles. These are often found in medical tents or pharmacies.
3.  **Punch Out I**: This is a cheap early-game skill (25 AP) that restores stamina on knockouts, making large brawls much easier to manage.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Tip">
The whip isn't just for swinging. In combat, use the whip to pull an enemy's legs or disarm them. This creates an opening for a one-hit knockout or a heavy melee strike.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="The Order of Giants DLC Guide">

The "Order of Giants" story DLC expands the narrative, taking Indy back to Rome to investigate the Nephilim Order. This content is set during the main campaign's timeline.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accessing the DLC">
    Ensure you have reached the "Bring the Parchment to Antonio" objective in the main story. Head to the Vatican Fountain of Confession and look for Father Ricci and his parrot, Pio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Villa Pia Grounds">
    Follow Pio to reach Father Ricci’s office. You will need to climb the exterior walls and secure a key to enter the Casina/Villa Pia grounds, which serves as the DLC's primary hub.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cult of Mithras Investigation">
    Navigate the underground ruins beneath Rome. This area is heavily guarded by the Nephilim Order. Use the "Depths of the City" routes to avoid direct confrontations with heavy units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="100% Completion">
    The DLC contains 118 Journal Notes and 7 Mithraic Artifacts. Ensure you collect the "Roman Scholar" and "The Seven Grades" notes to unlock the final DLC achievements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="DLC Rewards">
Completing the DLC rewards you with unique outfits and high-tier Adventure Books that can be used back in the main game campaign.
