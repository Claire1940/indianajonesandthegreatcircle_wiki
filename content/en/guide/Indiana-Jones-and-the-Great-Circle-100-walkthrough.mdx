<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Boss Strategies">

Combat in *The Great Circle* is a blend of brawling and tactical whip usage. On Very Hard difficulty, you cannot simply trade blows with enemies.

**Locus (The Giant) Strategy:**
Locus is impervious to standard punches. To defeat him in the Vatican and the Himalayas, you must use the environment. In the Vatican, lead him under the hanging chandeliers and use your whip to drop them. In the Himalayas, use the ship's narrow corridors to limit his movement and strike his unarmored legs.

**Emmerich Voss Strategy:**
Voss relies on his guards and long-range firearms. The key to this fight is staying mobile. Use the whip to pull environmental objects (like crates or explosive barrels) toward him. When he reloads, close the gap for a melee combo.

| Enemy Type | Weakness | Recommended Tool | Priority |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Black Shirt** | Stealth Takedown | Hands/Cosh | Medium |
| **Nazi Officer** | Headshots | Revolver | High (Calls Reinforcements) |
| **Swiss Guard** | Disarm & Counter | Whip | Low (Avoid if possible) |
| **Giant (Locus)** | Environmental Damage | Whip / Trap | Boss |

<Callout type="info" title="Whip Tactics">
The whip is not just for swinging. You can use it to trip running enemies or pull the helmets off armored soldiers, making them vulnerable to a single knockout blow.
</Callout>
