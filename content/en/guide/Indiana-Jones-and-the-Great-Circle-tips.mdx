<Section icon={Trophy} title="Character Progression and Adventure Books">

Progression in Indiana Jones and the Great Circle is tied to Adventure Books. These items are found throughout the world and allow you to spend Adventure Points (AP) or Medicine Bottles to unlock permanent upgrades. Choosing the right skills early can drastically change your effectiveness in both brawling and exploration.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Survival Essentials">
    Focus on unlocking **Lucky Hat I** immediately. This skill gives you a "second wind" if you are knocked out in combat, allowing Indy to regain health simply by picking up his hat. Pair this with **Punch Out I** to restore stamina during melee fights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Exploration and Fitness">
    Invest in **Climbing Ace I** and **Pep I**. These reduce stamina drain while climbing and increase the speed at which your stamina bar refills. Since most of the game involves vertical traversal, these are high-priority upgrades for any player.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combat Specialization">
    Once your survival is secured, look for **Throttle** and **Jumbo Lasso**. These allow you to use your whip to neutralize enemies silently or pull heavy-weight enemies into a grab, which is essential for the late-game encounters in Iraq.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Upgrade Strategy">
Don't hoard your Adventure Points. While some high-tier books cost upwards of 300 AP, the early-game benefits of increased stamina and health reserves far outweigh the wait for end-game abilities.
</Callout>

Remember that each region uses its own currency for vendors. Use your gathered coins in the Vatican to buy the **Vatican Books** and **Vatican Relics** exploration guides from Ernesto. These will mark missing collectibles on your map, making the cleanup process much more efficient.

</Section>

<Callout type="tip" title="Clue Hunting">
If a safe code isn't working, double-check the "leading zeroes." Some codes in the Great Circle are five digits long, and entering them into a four-digit lock requires careful attention to the specific dial you are turning.
</Callout>

In the Himalayas, the **Kummetz Cipher** is a multi-step process. You must find four code wheels and use the cipher machine to translate the word "BLUT" into the numerical code **3666**. This is a mandatory story gate, so ensure you have explored the officer's quarters thoroughly before attempting to pass.
