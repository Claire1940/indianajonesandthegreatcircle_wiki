<Section icon={Search} title="The Archivist: Collectible Mastery">

The most daunting part of the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle trophy guide** is the **Archivist** trophy. You must find 519 collectibles. Fortunately, the game provides in-game maps that you can purchase from vendors to help locate these items.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Purchase Area Maps">
    As soon as you reach a new hub like Vatican City or Giza, find the local vendor (often a "post office" or merchant) and buy all available maps. These will mark collectible locations on your HUD.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adventure Books">
    Collect Adventure Books to unlock skills. You need almost all of them for the Platinum. These boost Indy's health, stealth, and combat prowess.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Photography Mastery">
    Use your camera to snap 50+ photos of inscriptions, historical sites, and key characters. Antonio in the Vatican will help you translate these early on.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Lost Artifacts">
    Find all "Lost Artifacts." These are distinct from normal collectibles and are often hidden behind complex puzzles or in secret rooms within tombs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Collectible Type | Total Count | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Adventure Books** | ~30 | Unlocks new abilities and stat boosts. |
| **Photos** | 50+ | Required for story progression and specific trophies. |
| **Artifacts** | 50 | Used in the final puzzle to unlock the true ending. |
| **Mysteries** | Area Specific | Solving all in an area grants a Gold trophy (e.g., "When in Rome"). |
