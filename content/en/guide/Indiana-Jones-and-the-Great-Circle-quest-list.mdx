Combat in Indiana Jones and the Great Circle is as much about style as it is about survival. To maximize your efficiency, focus on specific combat achievements early in the game, as many can be completed within the first few areas.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Disarm and Neutralize">
    Use your whip to disarm an enemy, pick up their dropped weapon, and use it to knock them out to unlock the 'Your Own Medicine' achievement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Environmental Kills">
    Locate ledges early in the Vatican or Giza sections to push enemies off, fulfilling the 'A Little Tumble' requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mastering Counters">
    Focus on blocking and countering enemy strikes to earn 'Offensive Defense' without performing extra punches.
  </Step>
</Steps>
