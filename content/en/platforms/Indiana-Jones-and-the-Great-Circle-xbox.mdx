<Section icon={Map} title="Complete Story Walkthrough: The Great Circle Route">

The campaign of Indiana Jones and the Great Circle xbox is structured around 10 main Adventures. These take Indy from the familiar halls of Marshall College to massive "open-area" hubs like the Vatican and Sukhothai. Following the story naturally is the best way to unlock traversal tools like the lighter and improved whip grips.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Golden Idol (South America)">
    The opening tutorial. Learn basic traversal, how to avoid traps, and the fundamentals of the whip. Focus on escaping the temple alive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Break-In (Marshall College)">
    Investigate the museum theft. This is a narrative-heavy chapter. Be sure to collect the early **Journal Notes** in Indy's office before leaving for Italy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Stolen Cat Mummy (The Vatican)">
    The first large hub. You will meet Antonio and Ernesto. This area introduces **disguises**, which are essential for entering restricted military zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Idol of Ra (Gizeh)">
    A massive desert hub. You will solve the Light Chamber puzzles and explore the Great Pyramid. This is where you will spend the most time tracking **Stelae**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Blessed Pearl (Sukhothai)">
    A water-based hub. Use the boat to navigate flooded temples. You will need to collect **Khmer Cogwheels** to solve the final gear puzzles here.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Point of No Return">
While the game allows for "Free Roam" cleanup after the story via the **Travels** tab in your Journal, some specific dialogue-based Journal Notes can be missed if you rush through story beats. Always talk to named NPCs like Gina or Antonio before leaving a region.
</Callout>

The endgame takes place in **Iraq (Atonements)**. Once the credits roll, you can return to any previous hub to finish your "Archivist" trophy or find the remaining "Ancient Relics." The Secret Vault cleanup in Iraq is only possible after collecting all 50 Ancient Relics across the globe.

</Section>

To solve the Vatican's Fountain of Confession, turn the dragon statues to face the fountain, then whip the center dragon. For the baptism puzzle, move the man and boy into the water ring. For the boat puzzle, rotate the rings to clear a path for the miniature vessel.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Adventure Books and Character Progression">

In Indiana Jones and the Great Circle xbox, you don't level up in the traditional sense. Instead, you find **Adventure Books** hidden in the world. Once found, you spend **Adventure Points (AP)**—earned by taking photos and discovering landmarks—to unlock permanent skills.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Survival Essentials">
    - **Lucky Hat I**: Allows Indy to revive after a knockout by picking up his hat.
    - **Moxie I-III**: Permanently increases your total stamina bar.
    - **Shaping Up**: Adds extra health reserve bars for tough fights.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Combat & Stealth">
    - **Throttle**: Whip-pull unaware enemies for an instant silent knockout.
    - **Punch Out**: Restores stamina every time you land a successful finisher.
    - **Jumbo Lasso**: Allows the whip to pull heavyweight enemies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exploration Aids">
    - **Regional Maps**: Bought from vendors like Ernesto (Vatican) or Tongdang (Sukhothai).
    - **Climbing Ace**: Reduces stamina drain while ledge-hanging.
    - **Fruit Bag**: Doubles the amount of healing items you can carry.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="AP Farming">
The fastest way to earn AP is through **Photography**. Every time your camera icon flashes, take a photo of the landmark or inscription. Returning these photos to Antonio in the Vatican also unlocks the "Path of Junia" rewards.
</Callout>

Prioritize **Lucky Hat** and **Moxie** early in the Vatican. These provide a much-needed safety net for the more difficult combat encounters in Gizeh and the Himalayas. If you are a completionist, buy the **Vatican/Gizeh/Sukhothai Books** from vendors immediately; they reveal the locations of other upgrade books on your map.

</Section>
