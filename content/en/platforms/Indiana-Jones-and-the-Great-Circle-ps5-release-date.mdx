To get the most out of the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle ps5 release date**, players should adjust their console settings to match their display capabilities. The PS5 version includes several toggles specifically designed for the DualSense controller and the console's unique audio engine.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Haptic Feedback">
    Navigate to the Controls menu and set Haptic Intensity to "High." This allows you to feel the tension of the whip and the texture of different surfaces while climbing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure 3D Audio">
    Use the PlayStation Pulse Elite or any compatible headset. The game's spatial audio is crucial for locating guards and hearing the subtle click of trap mechanisms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Graphic Mode">
    Choose between **Fidelity** (Native 4K, 30FPS, Max Ray Tracing) or **Performance** (Dynamic 4K, 60FPS). On PS5 Pro, use **Pro Mode** for PSSR-enhanced 60FPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Calibrate HDR">
    Adjust the peak brightness settings to ensure the deep shadows of the tombs don't wash out the golden highlights of the artifacts.

</Steps>
