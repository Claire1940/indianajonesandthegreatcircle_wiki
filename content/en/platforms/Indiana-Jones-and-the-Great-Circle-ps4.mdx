<Section icon={Map} title="Complete Story Walkthrough and Regions">

The campaign of Indiana Jones and the Great Circle is structured around 10 main Adventures. These take you across the globe, combining linear cinematic sequences with large, open-area hubs like Gizeh and Sukhothai. Following a clean route is essential for players aiming for the "Archivist" trophy, which requires finding nearly 500 Journal Notes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Golden Idol & Marshall College">
    Begin with the tutorial in South America to learn basic traversal and traps. Once back at Marshall College, investigate the museum break-in. **Tip**: Collect all Journal Notes in Indy's office and the student lounge before departing for the Vatican.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Vatican Hub">
    This is your first major open area. Meet Antonio and Ernesto to acquire the camera. You must solve the Fountain of Confession puzzle to access the underground route. Ensure you buy the Vatican exploration books from Ernesto to mark collectibles on your map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gizeh Desert Exploration">
    In Egypt, the map opens up significantly. You will need to locate Nazi camps and dig sites. The "Idol of Ra" adventure involves complex light-based puzzles inside the pyramids. Prioritize the "Cloud Atlas" mystery to unlock the Gizeh Medicine book.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Himalayas & Shanghai">
    These are more linear chapters. On the KMS Kummetz, you must solve the Kummetz Cipher to progress. In Shanghai, focus on the high-pressure escape sequence while grabbing visible notes along the path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Sukhothai & The Final Push">
    The final hub in Thailand introduces boat traversal. You must collect 10 Khmer Cogwheels to solve the final gear puzzle. After finishing the story in Iraq, use the "Travels" tab in your Journal to return to any region for post-game cleanup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Unlike AP, which is earned through almost any action, Medicine Bottles are rare regional collectibles. You must bring them to local doctors (like Ernesto in the Vatican or Tongdang in Sukhothai) to unlock the highest tiers of health and stamina upgrades.
</Callout>
