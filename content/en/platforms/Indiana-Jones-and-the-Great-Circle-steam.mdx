<Section icon={Shield} title="Steam Deck Optimization Guide">

The **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle steam** experience extends to the Steam Deck, where the game has achieved "Verified" status. However, achieving a stable 30 or 40 FPS requires specific adjustments to the graphical presets. The handheld version focuses on visibility and battery life without sacrificing the core aesthetic of the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Preset Selection">
    Start by selecting the 'Low' global preset and then manually increase Texture Quality to 'Medium' if you are on the 512GB or OLED models.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resolution Scaling">
    Enable FSR 3.0 and set it to 'Balanced'. This provides a significant frame rate boost while maintaining a sharp image on the 800p display.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Frame Rate Limiting">
    Set the Steam Deck's internal refresh rate to 40Hz and limit the game to 40 FPS. This offers a much smoother experience than 30 FPS with better battery endurance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Shader Pre-Caching">
    Ensure you allow Steam to finish downloading shader pre-caches before launching to prevent stuttering during combat or cinematic transitions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Battery Optimization">
Lowering the 'Shadow Quality' to Low and disabling 'Screen Space Reflections' can grant an extra 20-30 minutes of playtime on a single charge without heavily impacting the visuals.
</Callout>

</Section>
