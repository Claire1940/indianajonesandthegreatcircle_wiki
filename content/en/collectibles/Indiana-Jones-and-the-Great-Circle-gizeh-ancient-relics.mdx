<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Solving the Chamber of Resonance">

The most significant hurdle for collecting relics during "The Idol of Ra" mission is the **Chamber of Resonance** located deep beneath the Great Pyramid. This puzzle is mandatory for story progression and rewards you with access to the underground cave system where multiple relics are stored.

**Puzzle Highlights:**
- Requires the use of Indy's camera to photograph Adamic tablets.
- Involves physical environmental manipulation using a two-handed mace.
- Requires a specific sequence of "Photos" to be selected in the correct narrative order.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Tablet Collection">
    Enter the chamber and photograph the visible Adamic tablets. You will notice a missing piece; crawl into the low tunnel nearby to find the fragment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Structural Repair">
    Use the two-handed mace found in the corner to break the weakened wall. Retrieve the tablet piece and place it back into the broken structure to complete the inscription.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Photo Sequencing">
    Open your journal and select the six photos in this exact order: **Heed**, **The Lord's**, **Oath**, **Protect**, **The secret**, **Of the name**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Activation">
    Once the sequence is correct, the resonance will trigger, opening the path to the Light Chamber and revealing the hidden Ancient Relics within the alcoves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Completion Reward">
Solving this puzzle not only progresses the main adventure but also marks the "Chamber of Resonance" Mystery as complete in your journal, granting a massive boost of Adventure Points.
</Callout>

</Section>
