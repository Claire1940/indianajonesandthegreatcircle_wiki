Many of the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle vatican ancient relics** are located in "Restricted Zones." If Indy is spotted in these areas without a disguise, guards will attack on sight. 

- **Disguises**: Look for priest robes or worker uniforms to blend into specific areas like the Apostolic Palace.
- **Whip Traversal**: Use overhead beams to bypass guards. The "Climbing Ace" skill reduces the stamina cost of these maneuvers.
- **Distractions**: Throw bottles or use the environment to lure guards away from safes and relic chests.
