### Detailed Location Breakdown
1.  **The Drinking Horn**: This is often the first artifact players find. It is locked inside a safe (Code: 7171) in a small storage room connected to the museum gardens. You can reach this during the "A Nun in Trouble" Fieldwork.
2.  **The Golden Chalice**: Located in the Apostolic Palace. You must solve a mechanical puzzle box by sliding levers and placing a medallion in the correct compartment.
3.  **Roman Coin Set**: Found in a military tent in the Belvedere Courtyard. You need to find a document regarding shipping dates to unlock the container (Code: 5238).
