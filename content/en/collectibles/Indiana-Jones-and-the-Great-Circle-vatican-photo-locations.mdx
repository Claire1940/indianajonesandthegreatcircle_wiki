<Section icon={Zap} title="The Final Climb: Fountain 4 and the Shortcut">

The fourth and final location is the most complex. It involves vertical movement and a specific path that can be confusing if you only rely on the in-game map. To reach this area, you must backtrack slightly from the third fountain toward where the Captain was stationed.

| Path Segment | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- |
| **The Wall** | Hop over the low wall near the Captain's post. |
| **The Corridor** | Run down the long alleyway past the parked truck. |
| **The Ladder** | Locate the ladder on the left side of the inner hallway. |
| **The Scale** | Climb and shimmy along the ledge to the upper fountain. |

After reaching the top of the ladder, you will find the final fountain on the right. This area also contains a **Medicine Bottle**—be sure to grab it to replenish your health before the next phase of the mission.

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Navigation">
Once you have the fourth photo, do not walk back the way you came. It is time-consuming and risky. Instead, look for a shortcut through the nearby open windows.
</Callout>

**The Zipline Shortcut:**
After taking the photo, turn around and look for a lower walkway with open windows. Climb down (carefully use your whip to avoid fall damage) and enter the room. Inside, you will find a "Mystery" puzzle involving sliding drawers and objects. Once done, exit the room to find a zipline. This zipline will take Indy directly back toward the main area, significantly cutting down the travel time to Antonio.
