<Section icon={Target} title="Key Puzzle Solutions in the Vatican">

The Vatican is home to some of the most intricate mechanical puzzles in the game. Solving these is the only way to progress the "Stolen Cat Mummy" adventure and uncover the deepest **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle vatican field notes**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fountain of Confession">
    Located in the Vatican Gardens. First, rotate the two dragon statues at the base so they face the central fountain. Use your whip on the center dragon to activate the mechanism. Rotate the fountain cube to align the baptism scene (move the boy and man into position). Finally, solve the boat side by rotating the rings to clear the path for the marble boat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sacred Wounds Crucifix">
    Found in the secret chamber beneath the Tower of Nicholas V. Pour wine into the golden basins to reveal residue numbers. Match these numbers to the specific "wounds" on the crucifix mural: Head (1), Hands (2), Side (3), and Feet (4). Pulling the corresponding levers in the order described in the nearby poem will open the path to the Secret Monastery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="House of God Puzzle Box">
    Located in the Apostolic Palace art gallery. Slide the front lever to reveal a hidden compartment. Move the medallion to the opposite side and take the Golden Chalice. Placing the chalice on top of the box triggers the final mechanism, rewarding you with the Vatican Relics exploration book.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Reward">
Completing the Fountain of Confession puzzle is a major milestone that unlocks the "Out of the Vatican" achievement and provides several high-value journal entries.
</Callout>
