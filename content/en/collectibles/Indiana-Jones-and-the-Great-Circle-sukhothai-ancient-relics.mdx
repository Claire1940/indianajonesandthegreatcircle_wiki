<Section icon={Target} title="Solving the Khmer Cogwheel Puzzles">

The Khmer Cogwheel circuit is the most complex collectible-based puzzle in Sukhothai. To unlock the final relic in this chain, you must locate 10 individual cogwheels hidden across the map and use them to repair ancient machinery at various sites.

**Step-by-Step Gear Completion:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect the 10 Cogwheels">
    Search the flooded huts and Nazi storage tents. Cogwheels are often found on workbenches or hidden behind breakable crates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Gear Sites">
    There are multiple sites, with the final and most important one located west of Voss's Camp. Each site requires a specific number of gears to function.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Connect the Train">
    Place the cogwheels on the pegs to connect the power source to the ancient door or pedestal. You may need to move gears between sites if you haven't found all 10 yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim the Relics">
    Each completed gear site typically rewards you with a minor relic or an Adventure Book. The final site unlocks a major Ancient Relic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Gear Head Strategy">
Don't try to solve the final gear puzzle until you have all 10 cogwheels in your inventory. The final site requires a complete chain that uses every single gear found in the region.
</Callout>

</Section>
