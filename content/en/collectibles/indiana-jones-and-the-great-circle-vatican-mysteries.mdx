<Section icon={Target} title="The Bulls of Blood: Castel Sant'Angelo's Secret">
This is often the first mystery players encounter, located within the Castel Sant'Angelo. It's partially story-related and involves deciphering clues from notes to reveal a hidden passage. The core puzzle revolves around manipulating paintings to align their imagery.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Notes">
    After a courtyard encounter with enemies in Castel Sant'Angelo, find the first mystery note on a table in a room with two sleeping guards and one patrolling guard. Collect the "Red Bulls card" and "Angel Courtyard map" from a nearby table.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Solve the Painting Puzzle">
    In the same room, observe two hanging frames. The objective is to **flip both paintings so that the white bull is at the bottom**. This action will reveal a secret doorway.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Your Reward">
    Enter the newly opened secret doorway to find the final note and complete "The Bulls of Blood" mystery. This rewards you with 100 Adventure Points.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Mystery Complete">
Successfully solving "The Bulls of Blood" unlocks 100 Adventure Points and provides valuable early game lore.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="House of God (Lever Puzzle): Belvedere Courtyard">
The "House of God" mystery takes you to the upper levels of the Belvedere Courtyard, accessible by climbing construction platforms or using your whip through alleyways. This mystery centers around a large white model of a building and a series of lever manipulations.

    - **Move lever left**: Creates an empty slot for the Medallion.
    - **Insert Medallion**: Opens another compartment with Golden Chalice.
    - **Move lever left again**: Final compartment reveals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Key Items & Rewards">

    - **Golden Chalice**: Found in the second compartment.

<Callout type="info" title="Vatican Relics Guide">
Completing the "House of God" mystery is highly recommended early on, as it provides the Vatican Relics collectible guide, making it infinitely easier to locate other valuable items throughout the Vatican.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Father and Son: Borgia Tower's Safe">
Accessible only after completing the "A Nun in Trouble" fieldwork (which grants Juliana's key), this mystery is located in a room next to the Borgia Tower. It involves deciphering a safe code using two cryptic notes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Room">
    Use Juliana's key to enter the room next to the Borgia Tower. This room often contains a cat and a dog and appears to be a surveillance operation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect the Clues">
    Locate and grab two essential notes: the **Polybius Square Diagram** and the **Italian Poem**. The Italian Poem will have several circled letters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Decipher the Safe Code">
    Combine the circled letters from the Italian Poem with the Polybius Square Diagram to form the safe code. The correct code is **5873**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Open the Safe">
    Input the code 5873 into the safe. Collect all items inside to complete the "Father and Son" mystery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Key Requirement">
Remember, access to this mystery is gated by completing the "A Nun in Trouble" fieldwork, which provides Juliana's key. Don't waste time trying to access it without this key.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Signs of Trouble: Gina's Message">
This mystery intertwines with Gina's storyline and requires you to retrieve information she has hidden. It begins in the Sistine Chapel and leads to an excavation site.

| Location           | Action Required                                  | Key Item/Code | Reward/Outcome                                  |
| :----------------- | :----------------------------------------------- | :------------ | :---------------------------------------------- |
| **Sistine Chapel** | Find Gina's note                                 | N/A           | Starts "Signs of Trouble" mystery               |
| **Excavation Site**| Locate a safe inside a tent                      | Safe Code: **1891** | Contact Message, Cash                           |
| **Sistine Chapel** | Return to the original note location, deposit message | N/A           | Mystery Complete, Adventure Points              |

Before heading to the Excavation Site, it's advisable to acquire the **Blackshirt disguise** to blend in, as it's a restricted area. The Blackshirt uniform can be found after meeting Gina on a balcony near the Sistine Chapel, zipping down, and going under a chain-link fence.

<Callout type="warning" title="Blackshirt Disguise Recommended">
The Excavation Site is a restricted area. Wearing the Blackshirt uniform will significantly ease your infiltration and avoid unnecessary combat.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Secret of Secrets: Sistine Chapel's Enigma">
Located in the southwest of the Vatican, within the Sistine Chapel, this mystery involves a safe and a cryptic letter.

| Clue Source         | Information                                       | Safe Code Segment |
| :------------------ | :------------------------------------------------ | :---------------- |
| **Nicoletti's Letter** | Found on a desk near the safe, hints at the code. | N/A               |
| **Saint Peter Statue** | Bottom of statue reveals symbols.                 | **44**            |
| **Saint Paul Statue** | Underneath statue reveals symbols.                | **71**            |
| **Planetary Chart** | Deciphers symbols into numbers.                   | N/A               |

The full safe code, derived from combining the numbers from the Saint Peter and Saint Paul statues and deciphering them with the Planetary Chart, is **4471**. Input this code into the safe to complete the "Secret of Secrets" mystery and retrieve a Hidden Note.

<Callout type="info" title="Decoding the Symbols">
The Planetary Chart is essential for translating the symbols found on the statues into their numerical equivalents. Without it, cracking the safe code will be impossible.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="A Snake in the Garden: Catholic Palace Bookshelf">
This mystery takes you into the restricted Catholic Palace. After dealing with guards, you'll need to solve a bookshelf puzzle in Ventura's office, where a Dragon statue resides.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Infiltrate the Catholic Palace">
    Access the Catholic Palace. If you have the Blackshirt uniform, you can walk through the front door. Otherwise, prepare for stealth or combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Ventura's Office">
    Find Ventura's office, identifiable by a Dragon statue. The mystery note is on his desk, describing four books in a bookshelf.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Decipher the Book Order">
    The note indicates books must be removed alphabetically, but their names are on the back of the note: F (Crown), L (Triangle), Q (Star), P (Wheel). The correct order is **2, 4, 1, 3** (referring to their physical positions from left to right on the shelf).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pull the Books">
    Pull the books in the sequence: **second from left, fourth from left, first from left, third from left**. This will reveal a hidden compartment and complete "A Snake in the Garden" mystery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Book Description | Starting Letter | Position (Left to Right) | Pull Order |
| :--------------- | :-------------- | :----------------------- | :--------- |
| Crown            | F               | 2nd                      | 1st        |
| Triangle         | L               | 4th                      | 2nd        |
| Star             | Q               | 1st                      | 3rd        |
| Wheel            | P               | 3rd                      | 4th        |

<Callout type="success" title="Bookcase Solved">
Successfully pulling the books in the correct order (2-4-1-3) completes the mystery, revealing hidden items and lore.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="A Date to Remember: Museum Wing Garden Safe">
The final Vatican mystery, "A Date to Remember," leads you to a restricted area on the north side of the Vatican, specifically within the Museum Wing Garden. This mystery involves finding a locked container inside a Blackshirt tent.

| Objective         | Location                       | Key Item/Code |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------- | :------------ |
| **Infiltrate**    | Restricted area, north Vatican | Blackshirt Uniform (recommended) |
| **Locate Tent**   | Museum Wing Garden             | N/A           |
| **Find Safe**     | Inside a Blackshirt tent       | Safe Code: **6380** |
| **Collect Notes** | Inside the safe                | Medicine Collectible Book |

You'll need the Blackshirt uniform for easier access to this highly guarded area. Once inside the tent, locate the safe and input the code **6380**. This will complete the mystery and grant you a valuable Medicine Collectible Book.

<Callout type="tip" title="Stealth or Force">
While the Blackshirt uniform simplifies infiltration, skilled players can attempt to sneak through or engage guards in combat to reach the tent and solve this mystery.
</Callout>

</Section>
