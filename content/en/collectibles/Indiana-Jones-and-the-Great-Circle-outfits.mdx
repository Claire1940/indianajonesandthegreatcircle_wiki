<Section icon={Map} title="Vatican City Outfits and Locations">

The Vatican is the first major open area where the disguise system becomes mandatory for progression. After the intense events at Castel Sant'Angelo, Indy must navigate a city teeming with both religious figures and hostile occupiers.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Priest Suit (Civilian)">
    - **Location**: Antonio's Office
    - **Key Item**: Priest Key
    - **Best For**: Exploring the Sistine Chapel and public squares.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Black Shirt (Military)">
    - **Location**: Vatican Courtyard (Wash Tent)
    - **Unique Perk**: Access to the Underground Boxing Ring.
    - **Weapon**: Melee-focused.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The Priest Suit is handed to you automatically by Antonio, making it your primary tool for the early game. The Priest Key is arguably the most used utility item in this region, granting access to various locked chambers within the holy city. The Black Shirt uniform, however, requires more effort.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Progress the Story">
    Complete the main quest objectives until you have your initial conversation with Gina.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Courtyard">
    After the cutscene, use the zip line to cross the main courtyard area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Wash Tent">
    Drop down into the area with three patrolling guards. Look for the laundry/wash tent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Infiltrate the Boxing Ring">
    Equip the Black Shirt to enter the secret underground boxing ring, which is otherwise inaccessible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Melee Advantage">
The Black Shirt is unique among military **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle outfits** because it provides a melee weapon instead of a pistol. This is perfect for players who prefer silent takedowns even in restricted zones.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Giza Plateau: Dig Sites and Deserts">

In Egypt, the heat is as dangerous as the enemies. The Giza map features wide-open spaces and deep excavations. To reach the heart of the mysteries, you will need to look like you belong on a construction crew or a high-ranking military unit.

| Outfit Name | How to Unlock | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dig Site Disguise** | Sphinx Side Mission or Dame Noal | Accessing Khafre Excavation |
| **Vermo Uniform** | Climb the Tower (Post-Quest) | Entering the Knuckle Duster Den |

The Dig Site disguise is a worker's outfit. While it doesn't come with a key, it is a hard requirement for the main story quest involving the Khafre excavation. You can obtain it early by meeting G under the Sphinx for a side mission.

<Callout type="info" title="Secret Fight Clubs">
Just like the Vatican's boxing ring, Giza features a hidden combat arena called the Knuckle Duster Den. You can only enter this area safely while wearing the Vermo Uniform.
</Callout>

To find the Vermo Uniform, you must first complete the primary excavation quest. Once finished, navigate to the specific tower marked on your map and climb to the top. The uniform is tucked away in a locker near the observation deck.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Sukhothai: Jungle Infiltration">

The final major location, Sukhothai, offers a different challenge. Unlike the previous hubs, this map relies heavily on boat travel. Because the environment is primarily a dense jungle or occupied camp, civilian disguises are non-existent here.

<CardGrid cols={1}>
  <Card title="Royal Army Outfit (Military)">
    - **Location**: Near Voss's Camp.
    - **Requirement**: Talk to Tong Dang to trigger the side quest.
    - **Utility**: Unlocks three specific high-security doors and allows free movement in enemy zones.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The Royal Army Outfit is found during a side quest where you are tasked with stealing Voss's gold stash. While it doesn't grant access to a secret fight club, its utility in bypassing the heavy jungle patrols is unmatched.

<Callout type="success" title="Stealth Mastery">
In Sukhothai, the Royal Army Outfit is essential for players who want to avoid the constant boat-to-shore combat loops. It allows you to walk directly into camp centers to interact with quest items.
</Callout>

</Section>
