Some **indiana jones and the great circle unlockables** require more than just finding a note; they involve multi-step environmental puzzles. Two of the most challenging involve the Sistine Chapel's "Secret of Secrets" and the Borgia Tower's "Father and Son" mysteries.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Secret of Secrets Statues">
    Inside the Sistine Chapel back office, locate the statues of Peter (with the key) and Paul (with the sword). Rotate both statues to find planetary symbols on their bases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Planetary Alignment">
    Match the symbols found on the statues to the planetary chart in the same room. The code is determined by the planets' distance from the sun. The resulting code is **4471**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Father and Son Poem">
    After completing 'A Nun in Trouble,' use Juliana's Key to enter the room near Borgia Tower. Loot the poem and identify the circled letters: **I, R, I, A, T, A, S, O**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Square Diagram Translation">
    Apply the circled letters to the square diagram found on the wall. Each letter combination corresponds to a digit, revealing the final solution of **5873**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
