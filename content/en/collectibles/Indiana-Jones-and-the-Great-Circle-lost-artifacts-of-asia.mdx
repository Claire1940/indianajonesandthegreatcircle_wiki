<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Khmer Cogwheel Puzzle Route">

A unique mechanic tied to the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle lost artifacts of asia** is the collection of Khmer Cogwheels. These ten gears are scattered across various gear-puzzle sites. Completing these puzzles is the only way to recover certain Ancient Relics and reach 100% completion in Sukhothai.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Initial Cogwheels">
    Search the smaller islands and flooded huts near the starting area. Several cogwheels are sitting on crates or partially submerged in shallow water.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Gear-Puzzle Sites">
    There are multiple sites where gears must be connected. Use your whip to place the cogwheels onto the spindles to restore power to ancient elevators or doors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Final Gear Puzzle">
    Once you have all 10 Khmer Cogwheels, head to the site west of Voss' Camp. This complex machine requires all gears to be placed in a specific sequence to unlock the final Asian relic.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect the Reward">
    Solving the final puzzle grants a large amount of AP and the "Gear Head" completion check in your Journal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solution">
If you are stuck on the final gear puzzle, ensure you have photographed the mural nearby. Indy's Journal will provide a schematic showing the required gear ratios for the mechanism to turn.
</Callout>
