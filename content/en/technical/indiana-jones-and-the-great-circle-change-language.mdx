<Section icon={Target} title="PC Language Change: Xbox Game Pass Method">
If you're playing *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle* through Xbox Game Pass on PC, the primary method for changing the game's language involves adjusting your Windows system settings. This approach ensures the game launches with your desired language.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access System Settings">
    Click on the **Start Menu** and navigate to **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Go to Time & Language">
    In the Settings window, select **Time & Language** from the left-hand menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Language & Region">
    Click on **Language & Region** to view your installed languages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add a Preferred Language">
    If your desired language is not listed, click **Add a language** and select it from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prioritize Your Language">
    Once added, click the three dots next to your preferred language and select **Move up** to place it at the top of the list. This sets it as your primary display language.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Relaunch the Game">
    Close the Xbox Game Pass app completely, then relaunch *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle*. The game should now be in your newly selected language.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Note for Audio/Subtitle Mix">
If you wish to have English audio with local language subtitles, a workaround is required. Temporarily change your system language to English, restart your PC, and download the English audio files. Then, locate the `en_US` sound folder within the game's content directory (`Xbox game app > Manage > Files > Browse > Indiana Jones and the Great Circle > Content > Base > Sound > External Sources > PC`), copy it to a safe location, and revert your system language. Finally, replace the sound files with your saved English audio.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="PC Language Change: Steam Method">
For players on Steam, the process might involve a combination of Steam's built-in options and potentially manual file editing if an in-game option is not available.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open Steam Library">
    Launch Steam and go to your **Library**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access Game Properties">
    Right-click on *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle* in your game list and select **Properties**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Language Tab">
    In the Properties window, look for a **Language** tab. If available, select your desired language from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Game Files">
    After changing the language, it's often a good idea to go to the **Local Files** tab and click **Verify integrity of game files...** to ensure all language-specific assets are downloaded.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manual Configuration (if needed)">
    If a language tab is absent or doesn't work, some games might have a configuration file within the game's installation directory (e.g., `Steam/steamapps/common/Indiana Jones and the Great Circle/settings.ini` or similar) where you can manually edit the language entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The ability to choose different languages for text and voiceover separately is not always an in-game feature. Some players have requested this from the developers, and it may be added in future updates.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Console Language Change">
On consoles (Xbox Series X|S), *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle* typically follows the system language of your console. There are usually no in-game options for language selection.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Go to Console Settings">
    Navigate to your console's **Settings** menu from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select System Language">
    Find the **System** or **Language & Location** option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Your Language">
    Select your preferred language from the available options.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Restart Console">
    Restart your console to apply the new system language settings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Launch Game">
    Start *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle*. The game should now reflect your console's chosen language.
  </Step>
</Steps>
