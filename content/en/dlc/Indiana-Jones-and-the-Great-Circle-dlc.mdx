<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and the Fedora Perk">

While the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle dlc** emphasizes brains over brawn, you will still find yourself facing off against Nazi patrols and cult members. The combat system remains largely unchanged from the base game, which is both a strength and a minor missed opportunity. The focus is on parrying incoming strikes and countering with Indy's fists or environmental objects.

The whip remains your most versatile tool. It can be used to disarm enemies, pull them closer for a finishing blow, or swing across gaps to gain a tactical advantage. The revolver is a powerful last resort, though the game encourages a more stealthy or melee-focused approach.
