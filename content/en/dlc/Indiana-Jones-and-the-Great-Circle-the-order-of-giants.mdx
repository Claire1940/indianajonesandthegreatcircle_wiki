<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Solving the Major Puzzles">

The puzzles are the undisputed highlight of **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle the order of giants**. Many players find these challenges to be more rewarding and intricate than those found in the base game. Two specific puzzles stand out: the Roman Water Puzzle and the Great Marble Maze.

| Puzzle Name | Type | Difficulty | Key Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Vatican Office** | Logic/Search | Moderate | Find the key and trigger the bird event. |
| **Roman Water Canal** | Environmental | High | Manipulate valves to raise/lower water for traversal. |
| **The Marble Maze** | Physical/Skill | Very High | Guide a flaming ball through a giant maze without losing it. |
| **The Pope's Secret** | Text-Based | High | Link physical puzzle pieces to obtuse textual clues. |

The Marble Maze requires a steady hand and a keen eye for physics. You must navigate a flaming sphere through a series of tilts and turns. If the ball falls or the flame is extinguished, the puzzle resets. The Roman Water Puzzle, on the other hand, is a classic logic gate challenge where you must visualize the flow of the canals to reach higher ledges.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and the Order of Giants Cult">

While puzzles take center stage, **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle the order of giants** does not shy away from conflict. You will encounter standard Italian soldiers, but the primary threat comes from the Order of Giants—a mysterious cult dressed in red robes. These enemies are more aggressive and utilize different tactics than the standard military forces.

The combat remains a mix of first-person brawling and light shooting. However, the DLC does not introduce radically new combat mechanics. Instead, it tests your ability to use the environment to your advantage.

**Enemy Types and Tactics:**
- **Italian Infantry**: Standard riflemen and brawlers. Best handled with stealth or quick whip-disarms.
- **Red-Roed Cultists**: Aggressive melee fighters who often ambush Indy in tight catacomb corridors.
- **The Mini-Boss**: A unique encounter in a visually stunning location. This fight relies on a "run and ranged attack" cycle.

| Combat Tier | Enemy Type | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Low** | Scout | Stealth takedown or whip pull. |
| **Medium** | Cultist | Parrying and environmental hazards (fire/pits). |
| **High** | Mini-Boss | Constant movement and utilizing ranged gadgets. |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Strategy">
Against the cultists, use the narrow corridors of the catacombs to your advantage. Bottlenecking them allows you to manage multiple attackers without being flanked.
</Callout>
