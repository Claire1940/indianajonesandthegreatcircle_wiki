<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Order of Giants: Puzzle Solutions and Mechanics">

Puzzles are the beating heart of **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle dlc 2**. MachineGames has implemented a "boss puzzle" philosophy, where major environmental challenges act as the primary hurdles instead of traditional combat encounters. These puzzles often involve the Arcanum Tripartitus—a cylinder split into three pieces that Indy must recover to open the doors to the ancient Order.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Cylinder Hunt">
    Locate the three pieces of the Arcanum Tripartitus hidden across Rome. Each piece is guarded by a specific elemental or logical puzzle themed around Roman history.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Decoding the Mural">
    Use the mural inside the Villa Pia to understand the alignment of the Nephilim giants. This requires matching the light patterns with the constellations found in the Tower of Winds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Snake Room Challenge">
    In the deep catacombs, you will encounter a room filled with snake-themed pressure plates. Follow the path indicated by Father Ricci’s cryptic notes to avoid triggering deadly traps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Alignment">
    Once all pieces are collected, return to the Tomb of Gibborim. Align the cylinder pieces with the central pedestal while fending off waves of cultists to unlock the inner sanctum.
  </Step>
</Steps>

One specific puzzle in the Cloaca Maxima has been noted by players for its difficulty. It involves manipulating water levels to raise a series of floating platforms. If you find yourself stuck, remember that the solution is often simpler than it appears—look for the yellow markings that indicate whip-grapple points or interactable levers hidden behind vines.

| Puzzle Name | Difficulty | Core Mechanic |

| **Nero’s Trial** | Medium | Light Reflection / Mirrors |
| **The Mithras Dial** | High | Mathematical Logic / Symbols |
| **Tiber Navigation** | Low | Physics-based Boat Handling |
| **The Giant’s Door** | Very High | Multi-stage Environment Interaction |

<Callout type="warning" title="Puzzle Warning">

Indy must face the Cult of Mithras, a fearsome group of zealots who believe they are the rightful heirs to the Nephilim legacy. These enemies utilize a mix of traditional firearms and ancient melee weapons, requiring players to stay mobile and use the environment to their advantage.
