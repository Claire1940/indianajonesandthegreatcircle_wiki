<Section icon={Map} title="Indiana Jones and the Great Circle Order of Giants Walkthrough">

The core of the DLC involves a linear yet exploration-heavy route through Rome's hidden history. Follow these steps to progress through the main story mission.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Vatican Investigation">
    After speaking with Father Ricci, follow his parrot, **Pio**, through the Vatican traversal points. You will need to climb toward the higher balconies to reach **Father Ricci's Office**. Inside, search for the **Villa Pia Key** and read the correspondence regarding the Nephilim Order. This office contains the first 5 of the 118 Journal Notes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Entering Villa Pia">
    Use the key to enter the **Casina / Villa Pia grounds**. This area acts as a mini-hub. You must avoid the guards (or take them out silently) and locate the entrance to the **underground crypts**. Look for a loose floorboard in the tool shed behind the main villa to find a secret passage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Roman Streets">
    The path leads out of the Vatican and into the **streets of Rome**. This section is more open, allowing you to explore local shops and alleyways. Your goal is to find the **House of the Gilded Cupid**, which serves as a front for the Cult of Mithras. Use your camera to identify cult symbols on the walls to track their movements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Cult of Mithras Base">
    Once inside the cult base, you will encounter more heavy-weight enemies. Use the **Jumbo Lasso** ability if you have it unlocked. Solve the **Seven Grades of Initiation** puzzle (detailed in the next section) to open the massive stone door leading to the final confrontation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Depths of the City">
    The final act takes place in the **Nephilim Ruins** deep beneath Rome. This is a gauntlet of traps and environmental puzzles. Use Indy's whip to swing across crumbling pillars and reach the **Heart of the Mountain**, where the final cinematic and boss encounter occur.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Stealth is Key">
Rome is crawling with both Fascist soldiers and Cultist guards. While Indy can hold his own in a fistfight, the DLC rewards stealthy play. Use the **Throttle** ability to take out guards quietly, as reinforcements in the narrow Roman alleys can quickly overwhelm you.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Key Puzzle Solutions">

The **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle order of giants walkthrough** wouldn't be complete without solutions to the DLC's unique "Giant-sized" puzzles. These are often more complex than the base game's riddles.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Father Ricci's Desk Cipher">
    - **Location**: Father Ricci's Office (Vatican).
    - **Clue**: The Bible verses marked on the wall.
    - **Solution**: Align the desk drawers to match the numbers **3-1-6-2**.
    - **Reward**: Opens a hidden compartment containing the **Villa Pia Key**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Seven Grades of Initiation">
    - **Location**: Cult of Mithras Base.
    - **Clue**: The seven statues lining the ritual hall.
    - **Solution**: Place the ritual items (Raven, Bride, Soldier, Lion, Persian, Sun-Runner, Father) in their respective niches based on the height of the pedestals.
    - **Reward**: Unlocks the path to the Nephilim Ruins.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
