<Section icon={Lightbulb} title="Activating the Initial Mirrors and Opening Doors">
The Light Chamber puzzle begins with one active mirror and two darkened ones. Your first task is to locate and activate these additional light sources to gain full control over the puzzle. This involves opening two sealed doors, each with unique symbol requirements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate the First Mirror">
    Upon entering the Light Chamber, you'll find one mirror already shining a light. Your immediate goal is to use this light to open the first sealed door. Look for the door on the left side of the chamber (as you face the main gate). It bears a symbol resembling a circle with lines in the corners.
    <br />
    Direct the active mirror's beam onto the top-right smaller circle on the Three Eyed Gate. This symbol matches the one on the left door, causing it to open.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Retrieve the Second Mirror">
    Head through the newly opened passage. Inside this room, you'll find another mirror. Grab it and redirect its light back towards the central chamber, aiming it at one of the main mirrors. This will provide you with a second functional light beam.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Open the Second Door">
    The second sealed door, located on the right side of the main gate, features a diamond symbol with lines through it. To open this, you'll need to combine symbols.
    <br />
    Use one mirror to shine light on the middle-right circle (the diamond shape) and the other mirror to illuminate the top-left circle (which features a plus sign, also present on the door). This combination will unlock the right door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Place the Third Mirror">
    Enter the room behind the second opened door. You'll find a pedestal for a mirror. Carry the mirror you just retrieved from the first room and place it in this pedestal. With all three mirrors now active, you are ready to tackle the main Three Eyed Gate puzzle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Mirror Mastery">
Successfully activating all three mirrors is the cornerstone of solving the Light Chamber. Take your time to align each beam precisely, as even slight misalignments can prevent doors from opening or symbols from activating.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Eye} title="Solving the Three Eyed Gate Puzzle">
With all three mirrors operational, the true challenge of the Light Chamber unfolds: deciphering the sequence for the Three Eyed Gate. Each of the three large "eyes" on the main gate requires a specific combination of three smaller symbols to be simultaneously illuminated.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Top Eye Combination">
    To activate the **Top Eye**, align your three mirrors to highlight:
    - **Middle Right** circle
    - **Top Left** circle
    - **Bottom Left** circle
  </Card>
  <Card title="Middle Eye Combination">
    For the **Middle Eye**, shift your mirrors to illuminate:
    - **Top Right** circle
    - **Middle Left** circle
    - **Bottom Left** circle
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bottom Eye Combination">
    Finally, for the **Bottom Eye**, position your mirrors to target:
    - **Middle Left** circle
    - **Middle Right** circle
    - **Bottom Right** circle
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Once you successfully illuminate a combination, that "eye" will activate, and you can then proceed to the next. The puzzle resets if an incorrect combination is attempted, so careful planning is key.

<Callout type="warning" title="Precision is Key">
The game requires precise alignment of the light beams. If a combination doesn't activate, slightly adjust the mirror positions until the symbols are fully illuminated. Refer to the symbols on the gate itself for visual confirmation.
</Callout>
