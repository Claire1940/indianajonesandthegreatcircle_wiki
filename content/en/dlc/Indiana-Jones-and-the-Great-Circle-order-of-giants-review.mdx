<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Enemy Encounters">

While the puzzles take center stage, Indiana Jones still has plenty of opportunities to use his whip and fists. The combat in the Order of Giants is typical of the Great Circle—a blend of first-person brawling, light platforming, and stealth. However, the expansion introduces a group of mysterious, violent red-roed cult members who add a layer of tension to the dark corridors of Rome.

| Enemy Type | Combat Style | Recommended Tactic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Italian Soldiers** | Ranged/Patrol | Stealth takedowns or whip disarms |
| **Red-Roed Cultists** | Aggressive Melee | Counter-brawling and environmental traps |
| **Cult Leaders** | Ranged Attacks | Constant movement and ranged counters |
| **Mini-Boss** | Repetitive Patterns | Run, ranged attack, repeat cycle |

The combat does not introduce radically new mechanics, but it utilizes the tight spaces of the catacombs to create frantic encounters. The stealth sections are particularly atmospheric, as you navigate through shadows to avoid being overwhelmed by the cult's numbers. Unfortunately, the final boss fight falls a bit flat compared to the rest of the experience. It relies on a "run and shoot" pattern that can feel repetitive by the time the credits roll.

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Repetition">
The final encounter in the DLC lasts a bit longer than necessary. Focus on your ranged attacks and keep moving to avoid the boss's predictable but high-damage patterns.
</Callout>
