Set chronologically between *Raiders of the Lost Ark* and *The Last Crusade*, the story follows Indy as he uncovers a global conspiracy, taking players from the halls of Marshall College to the sands of Giza and the snowy peaks of the Himalayas. By meticulously blending first-person action with intricate puzzle design, the developers have crafted a title that honors the franchise's legacy while providing a fresh, engaging interactive experience. This Indiana Jones and the Great Circle review explores why this journey is a must-play for fans, despite some minor technical hurdles on the latest hardware.
