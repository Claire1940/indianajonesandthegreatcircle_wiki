<Section icon={Target} title="Solving the Fountain of Confession">

The Fountain of Confession is perhaps the most famous part of the **indiana jones and the great circle basilica puzzle** chain. Located in the Vatican Gardens, this puzzle only becomes active after you return the parchment from Augur the Giant's tomb to Antonio. It acts as a gatekeeper to the Secret Monastery.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate the Centerpiece">
    Locate the two dragon statues flanking the fountain. Rotate both statues so they are facing the central fountain structure. Once aligned, use your whip to pull the center dragon's head, which reveals the internal mechanism.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Baptism Logic Gate">
    Rotate the fountain cube to the side depicting a baptism. You must move the boy and the man figures into their correct positions using the water ring. Align the figures so they match the religious iconography found in your Journal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Boat Path Logic Gate">
    Rotate the cube to the boat side. This requires rotating the concentric rings and pulling the side levers to clear a path for the miniature boat. The goal is to create an unobstructed circular current for the water to flow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Alignment">
    Once both sides are solved, the fountain will descend, opening the hidden staircase leading toward the underground monastery and the continuation of the Stolen Cat Mummy quest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stealth Alert">
The Vatican Gardens are heavily patrolled by guards. Before attempting the fountain puzzle, clear the immediate area or use the nearby bushes for cover. If you are spotted, the puzzle interaction may be temporarily disabled until you lose the heat.
</Callout>

</Section>
