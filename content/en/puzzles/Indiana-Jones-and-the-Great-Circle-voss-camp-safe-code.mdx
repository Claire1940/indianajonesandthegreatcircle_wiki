<Section icon={Shield} title="Stealth and Combat Strategies for Voss' Camp">

Voss' Camp is one of the most densely populated enemy zones in *Indiana Jones and the Great Circle*. Charging in with a revolver is rarely the best option, as the alarm system will quickly bring in reinforcements, including armored heavyweights. To reach the safes undisturbed, a stealthy approach is highly recommended.

The whip is your most versatile tool here. You can use it to swing across the rafters of the barracks, allowing you to bypass guards on the ground floor. Additionally, the "Throttle" ability (unlocked via a Vatican Adventure Book) allows Indy to knock out unaware enemies with a whip pull, which is perfect for clearing the safe rooms quietly.

<Callout type="warning" title="Alarm Systems">
Voss' Camp features several alarm bells. If a guard reaches a bell, the difficulty of the encounter spikes significantly. Prioritize disabling these bells or taking out guards near them first.
</Callout>

**Enemy Types in Voss' Camp:**
- **Lightweight Guards**: Easily distracted by tossed objects; susceptible to one-hit stealth takedowns.
- **Snipers**: Positioned on towers; have a long line of sight. Always take them out first using the whip or a silenced approach.
- **Heavyweights**: Cannot be taken down with standard stealth from the front. Require the **Jumbo Lasso** ability or environmental traps to neutralize.
