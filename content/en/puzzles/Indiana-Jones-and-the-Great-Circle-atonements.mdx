<Section icon={Map} title="Rescue Gina: Infiltrating the Nazi Camp">
Your first objective in the "Atonements" chapter is to infiltrate the Nazi excavation site and rescue Gina. Locus will create a diversion, but you'll still face numerous guards. There are multiple approaches to navigate the camp.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Reconnaissance">
    Upon gaining control, immediately take a photo of the **Excavation Office** where Gina is held. This is an Adventure Note. Slide down from your vantage point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Entry Point">
    You have two main ways to enter the camp:
    - **Pipe Tunnel:** To the right of the sealed gate, behind covered trucks, find a hatch leading to a pipe tunnel under the dog kennel tent. Here, you can find a **Mersu Recipe** Adventure Note. This path leads through a road with many patrolling guards.
    - **Scaffolding Pit (Recommended):** To the left of the main entrance, locate a large pit with shaky scaffolding. Jump across quickly as platforms crumble. On the other side, find a small building containing an **Excavation Camp Map** and a pistol. This path offers better stealth opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate the Camp Stealthily">
    After entering via the scaffolding pit, head west. You'll likely encounter a guard radioing for help; take him out quietly. Check his desk for a **Censored Letter** Adventure Note. Move past crumbling walls towards a barracks tent. Inside, you can bash open a locked armory for weapons. Outside, two off-duty guards by the showers can be stealthily neutralized. Check benches for **Horror Tales #4**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach Gina's Location">
    North of the showers is a mess area with another guard. After dealing with him, approach the south side of the excavation office. Take out a patrolling guard and peek into the windows. You can call out to Gina from the far-left window, but you'll need another way in. The room outside Gina's cell has a lone guard, an Officer, and another guard at the front door. Use distractions or combat to clear the area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Free Gina">
    Once the Officer is down, photograph the **Ziggurat Model** (Adventure Note) and find the **Storage Room Key** in the second room. Open Gina's door and untie her. She will insist on continuing the adventure with you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success">
Successfully rescuing Gina unlocks a new dynamic for the rest of the chapter, with her assisting in certain puzzles and combat scenarios.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Ascending the Ziggurat: Puzzles and Progression">
With Gina by your side, your next goal is to reach the Ziggurat and uncover its secrets. This involves navigating more Nazi patrols and solving environmental puzzles.

### Path to the Ziggurat
After escaping the office, you'll spot the Ziggurat in the distance, guarded by Nazis.
- Take a photo of the **Ziggurat of Ur** from the front area by sneaking along two tents to the east.
- Stick to the far left to bypass most guards. Wait for an armored Nazi to cross a bridge, then move behind armored vehicles to take out two guards facing away.
- Climb a ruined stair and jump to scaffolding. Boost Gina up so she can move a crane for you to follow.
- At the top, photograph the **Nazi Digsite** (Adventure Note).
- Two Nazis will exit a lift; you can avoid them or push them off the ledge.
- Before heading down, find **Excavation Plans** (Adventure Note) on a table near a lone guard at the corner of the site.

### Ziggurat Interior
The Ziggurat's interior presents several mechanical puzzles.

| Task | Description | Key Item/Action |
| :--- | :---------- | :-------------- |
| **First Lift** | Take the lift down with Gina, but it stops on a high platform. | Find **Nineveh Document** (Adventure Note) between lifts. |
| **Powering Second Lift** | The second lift is unpowered. | Sidle past the lift, cross a fallen ladder, zipline to a lower platform. |
| **Creating a Bridge** | Reach a dead end on lower platforms. | Use whip on a large wooden wall to create a bridge. |
| **Activating Power** | Climb a ladder on the far side. | Find a junction box next to a generator to power the lift. |
| **Clearing Obstruction** | The lift gets caught before reaching the bottom. | Exit lift using whip, swing to far side, drop below lift. |
| **Final Descent** | Yank out wooden beams blocking the lift's descent with your whip. | Gina sends the lift down; climb on to reach the ground floor. |

<Callout type="info">
Once on the ground floor, find the **Ziggurat Guide** Adventure Book in the center of the room. This final book lists the locations of remaining collectibles.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Secret Vault and Final Confrontation with Voss">
Beneath the Ziggurat, you'll discover a monumental secret, leading to the final showdown with Emmerich Voss.

### The Secret Vault
To the right of the Ziggurat's exit, a large tunnel leads to the **Secret Vault**.
- Photograph the **Secret Vault** (Adventure Note).
- This puzzle requires **all 50 Ancient Relics**. While you'll likely have 49 at this point, the final one is collected post-game.
- You can attempt the puzzle with 49 relics, but it's recommended to complete the game first to avoid potential bugs and ensure you get the "Shadows out of Time" achievement.

| Door Section | Required Relics |
| :----------- | :-------------: |
| **First Door** | 11 |
| **Second Door** | 12 |
| **Third Door** | 13 |
| **Fourth Door** | 14 |

<Callout type="tip">
To solve the Ancient Relic puzzle, turning a relic left forms a box, while turning it right fills holes in all four directions until it hits a gap. This requires careful alignment.
</Callout>

### Getting to the Tent and Noah's Ark
After uncovering the Ziggurat's secret, Indy plans to head to a tent at the edge of the excavation yard.
- Photograph the giant **Noah's Ark** (Adventure Note).
- The main road to the tent is heavily guarded.
- Sneak past guards by using the narrow path to the right, past a sitting guard you can knock out.
- Use boxes and rocks for cover, then use your whip to climb a ledge, bypassing the guarded gate.
- Take out the last guard inspecting trucks and find the **Ark Analysis Note** (Adventure Note) on a smashed crate near the entrance gate.
- Stock up on food for health and stamina before entering the tent.

### Boss Battle: Emmerich Voss
Voss will catch you off-guard, leading to a hand-to-hand combat sequence.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Voss's Attack Patterns">
    - **Chopping Attacks:** Comes from either direction, varying speed. Block with one hand, punch with the other.
    - **Karate Kid Kick:** Cannot be blocked. Dodge to his side during his forward lunge to counter.
    - **Grab Attack:** Raises both hands before staggering. Quick, hard to dodge, but mash attack buttons to shake him off.
    - **Three-Hit Combo:** Transitions from chops, ends with a powerful attack. If you can't block the first, dodge the final hit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Combat Strategy">
    - **No Weapons:** You won't have your gun or whip; rely on fists.
    - **Block & Counter:** Wait for chopping attacks, block, then land 3 rapid punches.
    - **Dodge & Punish:** For kicks, dodge to his side and hit him while he recovers.
    - **Stamina Management:** Allow stamina to recover between exchanges.
    - **Final Phase:** You'll be tied to Voss over the Ark. Kick him away, block chops. Gina will intervene. Look up and grab her hand to escape, leaving Voss to his fate.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success">
Winning the fight isn't strictly necessary as Gina will ultimately save you. Focus on surviving and following the contextual prompts.
</Callout>

### Unlocking the Secret Vault
- **50th Ancient Relic:** Return to the tent where Voss captured you. It's now accessible. The final Ancient Relic is on the main table through the newly opened tent door.
- **Stone Research Note:** In the second room of Voss's tent, find a note listing rock types of each stone.
- **Secret Vault Puzzle:** With all 50 Ancient Relics, solve the Secret Vault puzzle. This triggers a special cutscene, an interesting post-credits scene, and automatically adds **The Great Circle Mural** to your notes.
- For a detailed guide on solving the Secret Vault, refer to our comprehensive **Guide to Unlocking the Ziggurat Secret Vault**.

<Callout type="info">
