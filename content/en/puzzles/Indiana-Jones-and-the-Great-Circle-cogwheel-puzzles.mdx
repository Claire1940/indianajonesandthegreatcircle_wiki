<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Mechanics">

Solving the puzzles requires more than just placing wheels. You must ensure that the gears engage correctly; sometimes, gears may appear to touch but are actually misaligned.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Area">
    Light up nearby torches to gain better visibility, as these underground chambers can be quite dark and difficult to navigate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Place the Gears">
    Insert your mini cog wheels into the board. Avoid placing gears that spin in opposite directions directly against each other, as this will lock the mechanism.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fine-tune Spacing">
    Check the gaps between gears carefully. If a puzzle isn't turning, the most common issue is a slight misalignment that prevents the teeth from catching.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate the Lever">
    Once the gear train is set, pull the lever to open the gate and claim your reward.
  </Step>
</Steps>
