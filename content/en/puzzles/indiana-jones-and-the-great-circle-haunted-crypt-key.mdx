<Section icon={Key} title="How to Get the Indiana Jones and the Great Circle Haunted Crypt Key">

Obtaining the **indiana jones and the great circle haunted crypt key** is a multi-step process that involves solving environmental riddles and utilizing Indy's camera. The key is not simply sitting in a chest; it is tied to the "Bright Future" and "The Haunting" mysteries. You will need to interact with specific NPCs and find hidden notes to trigger the key's appearance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start the Haunting Mystery">
    Head to the excavation area west of the Great Sphinx. You will find a discarded journal near a collapsed pillar. Photographing this note will officially begin "The Haunting" mystery in your Journal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Infiltrate the Nazi Vehicle Garage">
    The key is rumored to be held by a high-ranking officer stationed at the Nazi Vehicle Garage. Infiltrate the garage and locate the office on the second floor. Look for a clipboard titled "Excavation Transfer Logs."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Officer's Lockbox">
    The logs point to a hidden lockbox located in the Meteorological Station. Travel northwest and use the code **0609** (derived from the Cloud Atlas puzzle) to enter the station's storage room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve the Haunted Crypt Key">
    Inside the storage room, you will find a small iron key sitting atop a stack of meteorological maps. This is the **indiana jones and the great circle haunted crypt key**. Pick it up to update your objective.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Step">
The path between the Vehicle Garage and the Meteorological Station is patrolled by snipers. Stick to the canyons and use your whip to swing across gaps to stay out of their line of sight.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Solving the Crypt Puzzles">

Once you have the **indiana jones and the great circle haunted crypt key**, return to the "Seat of Eternity" tomb. The key opens the heavy stone door at the back of the first chamber. Inside, you will face a series of light-based puzzles and pressure plates that reflect the "Chamber of Resonance" mechanics found elsewhere in Gizeh.

| Puzzle Name | Requirement | Solution Summary | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Sun's Path** | Mirror Reflector | Align three mirrors to hit the central crystal | Opens Inner Sanctum |
| **Weight of History** | Two-handed Mace | Smash the cracked floor to reveal the lever | Ancient Relic |
| **Silent Guardians** | Camera | Photograph the four wall inscriptions | Mystery Completion |

The most difficult part of the crypt is the final light puzzle. You must use your mirror to reflect a beam of light through a series of dusty lenses. If the light is blocked by cobwebs, use your lighter to clear the path. This puzzle is essential for reaching the **Ancient Relic** hidden in the sarcophagus.
