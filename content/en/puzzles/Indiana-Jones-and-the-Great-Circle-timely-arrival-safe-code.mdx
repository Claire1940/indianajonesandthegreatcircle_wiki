The puzzle relies on a specific Italian cipher. Since the date is Saturday, you must look for the "Sab" column in the lookup table, which stands for "Sabato."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Day">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Table">
    Open the code lookup table and locate the column labeled "Sab" (Sabato).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Map the Letters">
    Find the numerical values associated with each letter of the word "guer" under the "Sab" column.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter the Code">
    Combine these numbers to form the final 5-digit sequence: 59404.
  </Step>
</Steps>
