<Section icon={Search} title="Decoding the Adamic Tablets">

The core of the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle tomb of gibborim puzzle** lies in the Adamic Tablets. These stone slabs contain the "Secret of the Name," and they are often scattered across the immediate vicinity of the puzzle room. You must find the missing pieces to complete the mural before the interaction prompt becomes active.

| Tablet Fragment | Location within Tomb | Visual Clue |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Heed** | Low tunnel to the left of the entrance | Eye symbol |
| **Protect** | Behind the breakable wall (use Mace) | Shield symbol |
| **The Secret** | Upper ledge near the hanging cage | Keyhole symbol |
| **The Lord's** | Submerged alcove in the water room | Crown symbol |
| **Oath** | Hidden behind the central sarcophagus | Hand symbol |

<Callout type="tip" title="Archaeology Tip">
Use the camera to photograph every tablet you find. Indy will automatically add these to his Journal, which allows you to review the symbols without backtracking to the physical objects.
</Callout>

Once all fragments are placed back into the mural, the "Resonance" mechanic begins. This requires you to select photos from your Journal in a specific narrative order that tells the story of the Gibborim (the Giants).

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Puzzle Solution">

Follow these precise steps to clear the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle tomb of gibborim puzzle** without triggering the trap reset. If you hear a deep rumbling sound, you have made an incorrect selection and must start the sequence over from the beginning.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Mural">
    Locate the broken section of the wall on the north side of the chamber. Place the **Adamic Tablet fragments** you collected. Indy will comment when the mural is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Photograph the Inscriptions">
    Equip your camera and zoom in on each of the six major symbols on the mural. You must have all six "Adamic Photos" in your Journal before you can interact with the central altar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Sequence of the Giants">
    Interact with the altar to open the selection menu. Choose your photos in this exact order:
    1. **Heed**
    2. **The Lord's**
    3. **Oath**
    4. **Protect**
    5. **The secret**
    6. **Of the name**
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Alignment">
    After the sequence is entered, the floor will rotate. Use your whip to grab the overhead beam and swing to the newly opened passage before the timer expires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved">
