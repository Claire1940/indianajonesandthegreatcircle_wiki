<Section icon={Target} title="Deciphering the Safe Combination">
Unlocking the safe requires a keen eye and a bit of deductive reasoning, as the combination is hidden within the environment and a critical document. The clues are scattered, hinting at a four-digit code.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find Nicol's Letter">
    On a table near the safe, locate and read **Nicol's letter**. This letter contains a vital hint: "If you've forgotten the combination again, look towards the stars and the Saints Peter with the key, then Paul with the sword."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Examine the Peter Statue">
    The Peter statue is located nearby and displays two symbols. One of these symbols, a smudged out number four, is not on the planetary chart, indicating it's the first digit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Planetary Chart">
    Around the same table, open a drawer to find a **planetary chart**. This chart shows numbers 1, 2, 3, and 4, and explicitly states it contains all planets except Pluto, meaning the number nine is excluded from the combination.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Analyze the Paul Statue">
    The Paul statue, positioned next to the safe, also features two symbols. The second symbol from the top, an arrow with a dot in the center of a circle, corresponds to the number one on the planetary chart. This gives you the last digit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Determine the Third Digit">
    With the first two digits (4 and 4) and the last digit (1) identified, the third digit needs to be found. Through a process of elimination or trial and error with numbers not on the chart (5, 6, 7, 8), the number **7** is revealed to be the missing digit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Combination Logic">
The planetary chart explicitly excludes Pluto, meaning the number 9 is not part of the combination. Additionally, the chart only displays numbers 1-4, implying that 0 is also not used. This narrows down the possibilities for the unknown digits.
</Callout>

Here's a breakdown of how the combination is derived:

| Clue Source        | Symbol/Hint                                      | Corresponding Number | Position in Combination |

| Planetary Chart    | "All planets except Pluto" (no 9), no 0          | Excludes 0 and 9     | General rule            |
| Paul Statue        | Arrow with dot in circle (second from top on chart) | **1**                | Last digit              |
| Elimination        | Remaining possibilities (5,6,7,8)                | **7**                | Third digit             |
