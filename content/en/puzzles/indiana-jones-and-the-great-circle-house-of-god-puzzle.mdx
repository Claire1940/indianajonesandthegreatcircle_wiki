<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solution for the House of God Puzzle">
Solving the **House of God puzzle** requires a precise sequence of interactions with the puzzle box. Follow these steps carefully to reveal its hidden mechanisms and retrieve the Golden Chalice.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Puzzle Box">
    Upon finding the puzzle box in the art gallery, your first action is to interact with it. Locate the **front lever** on the puzzle box. Slide this lever to begin the puzzle sequence. This action will usually reveal a hidden compartment or mechanism within the box.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Retrieve the Medallion">
    After sliding the front lever, a **medallion** will be revealed. This medallion is a key component. Carefully move this newly revealed medallion into the opposite compartment within the puzzle box. This action is crucial for the next stage of the puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Golden Chalice">
    Once the medallion is in its correct position, a **Golden Chalice** should become accessible. This chalice is the primary object you need to interact with to complete the puzzle. Look for it either within the box itself or nearby, now that the mechanism has shifted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Place the Golden Chalice">
    With the Golden Chalice in hand, the final step is to place it on top of the puzzle box. This action signifies the completion of the **House of God puzzle**. Upon successful placement, the puzzle will resolve, and you'll receive your reward.
  </Step>
</Steps>
