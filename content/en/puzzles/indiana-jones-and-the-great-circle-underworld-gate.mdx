<Section icon={Map} title="Solving the Adamic Tablets Puzzle">
The core of the Underworld Gate puzzle within the Chamber of Resonance revolves around the Adamic tablets. These ancient artifacts hold the key to unlocking the gate. Indy must first locate all the tablets and then arrange them in the correct sequence to activate the mechanism. This isn't just about finding them, but understanding their context.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Photograph the Initial Adamic Tablets">
    Upon entering the Chamber of Resonance, you will find several Adamic tablets. Use Indy's camera to photograph these tablets. This action often logs important clues into your Journal, which can be reviewed at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Missing Tablets in the Low Tunnel">
    Crawl into the low tunnel adjacent to the main chamber. This often hidden passage contains additional, crucial tablet pieces that are necessary for completing the puzzle. Be thorough in your search within this confined space.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Break the Wall and Reconstruct the Tablet">
    Within the low tunnel, you'll encounter a weak wall. Use a two-handed mace or a similar heavy object to break through it. This reveals a broken tablet. Place the tablet piece you found earlier back into this broken tablet to complete it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Select the Correct Sequence of Tablets">
    Once all tablets are gathered and reconstructed, you'll need to select them in a specific order based on the inscriptions and clues found. The correct sequence is: **Heed, The Lord's, Oath, Protect, The secret, Of the name.**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Pitfall">
Many players get stuck trying to force solutions without all the pieces. Make sure you've collected *all* tablet fragments, especially those in the low tunnel, before attempting to input the sequence. Missing even one piece will prevent progression.
</Callout>

</Section>
