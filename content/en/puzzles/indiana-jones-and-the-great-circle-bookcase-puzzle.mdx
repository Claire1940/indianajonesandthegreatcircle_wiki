<Section icon={Target} title="Secret of Secrets: Bookcase Puzzle Solution">

The "Secret of Secrets" is the definitive **indiana jones and the great circle bookcase puzzle** that most players search for. It involves decoding a four-digit combination by correlating saint statues with numerical symbols found in the office's research notes. The office is located just off the main corridor of the Sistine Chapel, and you will know you are in the right place when you see a large, locked safe embedded in the wall behind a desk.

  <Step num="1" title="Collect the Research Notes">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Inspect the Statues">
    There are four small statues placed around the room, often on the bookcases themselves. Use Indy's magnifying glass or camera to identify which saint each statue represents (Peter, Paul, etc.) and note the symbol etched into their base.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Decode the Combination">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Father and Son: The Borgia Tower Library">

Another complex **indiana jones and the great circle bookcase puzzle** occurs during the "Father and Son" mystery. This puzzle is located in a private study outside the Borgia Tower. Unlike the Sistine Chapel office, this room requires specific items obtained from the "A Nun in Trouble" side quest. The solution involves a Polybius Square diagram and an Italian poem, making it one of the more "archaeological" riddles in the game.

To solve this, you must examine the bookshelves for a hidden compartment triggered by the arrangement of books or the use of a key. The safe in this room contains high-value relics and notes that provide deep lore regarding the Great Circle's origins.
