The Sacred Wounds puzzle is a critical part of your journey through the Underworld in *Indiana Jones and The Great Circle*. It involves a series of body parts that need to be aligned with specific numeral positions, based on the wounds inflicted by the Spear of Longinus on Jesus Christ, but presented in a mirrored fashion from your perspective. Having your in-game notes handy is crucial, as they contain the numerical correspondences for each body part.

<Callout type="warning" title="Perspective Shift">
Always remember that the body you are interacting with is a mirror image. What appears as its left side is actually its right from your perspective, and vice-versa. This is a common pitfall for players attempting the Indiana Jones and The Great Circle Underworld puzzle.
</Callout>

The puzzle essentially asks you to interpret historical wounds and apply them to a symbolic representation. The Spear of Longinus is a central artifact, historically believed to have pierced Christ's side. This acts as your starting point, leading you to deduce the positions of other wounds.
