### Step-by-Step Deciphering Process

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Documents">
    Inside the room with the chest, find the **"Numeral Basics" note** on a shelf and the **"Egyptian Numeral Code" clipboard**. These two items are your primary references.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Understand Numeral Basics">
    Examine the "Numeral Basics" note. This document illustrates the symbols used in ancient Egyptian numerals and their corresponding numerical values. Pay close attention to the symbols that are circled or highlighted, as these are the ones you'll need for the chest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Count and Convert">
    Refer to the "Egyptian Numeral Code" clipboard. On this document, you will see a series of Egyptian numeral symbols. Your task is to count how many times each specific symbol (as identified in the "Numeral Basics" note) appears. The count of each symbol, in order, will form the digits of the code. For example, if a symbol representing '3' appears once, a symbol representing '2' twice, etc., you'll form the code.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Input the Code">
    Once you have determined the four-digit code, approach the Egyptian Numeral chest and input the combination. The correct **Egyptian Numeral code** is **3262**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
