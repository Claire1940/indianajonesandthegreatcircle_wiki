<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Solution for the Exhibit Puzzle">
Solving the Marshall College Exhibit Puzzle, particularly the **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle Egyptian statue puzzle** component, requires careful matching of artifacts to their corresponding display stands. Each stand features visual clues, such as flags, images, or descriptions, that indicate which artifact belongs there.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Bastet Statue">
    Find the **Bastet Statue** on the ground near the Egyptian exhibit. This statue depicts a cat-headed god, which is your primary clue.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Place the Bastet Statue">
    Place the **Bastet Statue** on the display stand featuring the **Egyptian flag** (green with a crescent moon and three stars). Specifically, look for the stand on the left with a **picture of the cat-headed god**. This completes the core **Indiana Jones and the Great Circle Egyptian statue puzzle** part.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify the Terracotta Relief">
    Pick up the **Terracotta Relief**. Its design suggests a Mesopotamian origin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Place the Terracotta Relief">
    Place the **Terracotta Relief** on the stand marked with the **flag of Iraq** (black, white, and green stripes with a red trapezoid containing two stars). Look for images of **similar-looking reliefs** next to where the Bastet Statue goes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Find the Ivory Case">
    Locate the **Ivory Case**. Notice the distinctive **Ugaritic letters** inscribed on its side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Place the Ivory Case">
    The **Ivory Case** is from Syria. Place it on the stand with the **Syrian flag** (three red stars in the middle). Search the far right side of the display for a **picture showing the lettering** to confirm its spot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Position the Funerary Mask">
    Retrieve the **Funerary Mask**. Marcus might comment on its solid gold composition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="8" title="Place the Funerary Mask">
    Also originating from Syria, the **Funerary Mask** belongs on the display depicting other **priceless jewels**. This stand is typically in the middle of the display, to the left of a noticeably empty Egyptian display case, which hints at the missing artifact.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved!">
Once all five artifacts are correctly placed, Indy will deduce that the missing item is a **Cat Mummy** from a dig in Siwa, Egypt, setting the stage for the next adventure.
</Callout>
