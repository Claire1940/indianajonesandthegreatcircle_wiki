<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Sacred Wounds Puzzle">
The Sacred Wounds puzzle in Indiana Jones and the Great Circle is centered around a large depiction of Jesus on the cross, flanked by a series of levers. Each lever corresponds to a specific body part and can be adjusted to numbers ranging from 1 to 4. The key to solving this puzzle lies in deciphering the clues provided by murals scattered throughout the surrounding area. These murals illustrate where Jesus was injured, and beneath them, basins (known as 'pacinas') reveal a number when wine is poured into them.

<Callout type="tip" title="Puzzle Overview">
The core mechanic involves **matching the injury locations** depicted in the murals with the corresponding numbered levers on the central Jesus figure. Pay close attention to both the body part highlighted in the mural and the number revealed in the pacina.
</Callout>

The puzzle's design encourages exploration and careful observation. Each mural tells a part of the story, guiding you to the correct settings for the levers. This blend of historical context and interactive gameplay makes the Sacred Wounds puzzle a memorable challenge within the game.
