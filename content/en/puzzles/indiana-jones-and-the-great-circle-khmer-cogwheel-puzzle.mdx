Successful completion usually triggers a reveal, granting access to an Ancient Relic or progressing a regional Mystery.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Solving Individual Cogwheel Puzzles">

Each time you find a Khmer Cogwheel and approach a corresponding gear train, you'll initiate a small puzzle. The goal is to connect all the gears so they can transmit motion from a power source to a final output mechanism, often a door or a hidden compartment.

### Puzzle Mechanics

The individual cogwheel puzzles are generally straightforward:

1.  **Place the Cogwheel:** Insert the collected cogwheel into the empty slot in the gear train.
2.  **Rotate for Alignment:** Once placed, you can often rotate the cogwheel. The goal is to align its teeth with the adjacent gears. You'll know it's correct when all gears visibly connect and the mechanism appears ready to turn.
3.  **Activate:** After proper alignment, a prompt will usually appear to activate the mechanism, revealing your reward.

| **Gear Alignment**   | Rotate cogwheels to connect teeth.               | Gears visibly interlock and turn.                    | Mechanism activation.    |

| **Collect Relic**    | Pick up the revealed Ancient Relic.              | Relic added to inventory, progress toward "Shadows out of Time". | Completion progress.     |

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Insert Remaining Cogwheels">
    Interact with the central mechanism. You will be prompted to insert the remaining Khmer Cogwheels. These will populate the larger gear train.

  </Step>
